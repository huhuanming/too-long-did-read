# Claude 非营利组织计划
# Claude for Nonprofits

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-nonprofits
> 日期: 2025-12-02

---

## English

Anthropic has launched Claude for Nonprofits in partnership with GivingTuesday, a global generosity movement. The initiative addresses the resource constraints faced by organizations tackling society's most challenging problems.

### Key Components

The program includes three main elements:

**Discounted Access:** Nonprofits are now eligible for discounts up to 75% on Team and Enterprise plans. These plans provide access to Claude Opus 4.6, Claude Sonnet 4.5, and Claude Haiku 4.5. The Team plan suits smaller organizations seeking collaborative features, while the Enterprise plan addresses larger organizations requiring advanced security and administrative controls.

**New Connectors:** Claude now integrates with three nonprofit-specific tools: Benevity (accessing 2.4 million validated nonprofits), Blackbaud (providing CRM and fundraising functionality), and Candid (offering grants and philanthropic opportunity data).

**Free Training Course:** "AI Fluency for Nonprofits" teaches staff to leverage AI for grant writing, program evaluation, donor engagement, and organizational efficiency. The curriculum requires no technical background.

### Implementation Partners

Anthropic collaborates with The Bridgespan Group, Idealist Consulting, Vera Solutions, and Slalom to support nonprofit adoption through strategic guidance and organization-wide implementation.

### Real-World Applications

Organizations like the Epilepsy Foundation, International Rescue Committee, and IDinsight demonstrate measurable impacts, including "surveys field-ready 16× faster" and accelerated humanitarian response capabilities.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 与全球慷慨运动 GivingTuesday 合作推出了 Claude 非营利组织计划。该倡议旨在解决应对社会最具挑战性问题的组织所面临的资源限制。

### 关键组成部分

该计划包括三个主要元素：

**折扣访问：** 非营利组织现在有资格获得团队版和企业版计划高达 75% 的折扣。这些计划提供对 Claude Opus 4.6、Claude Sonnet 4.5 和 Claude Haiku 4.5 的访问。团队版适合寻求协作功能的小型组织，而企业版则满足需要高级安全和管理控制的大型组织。

**新连接器：** Claude 现在与三个非营利特定工具集成：Benevity（访问 240 万个经过验证的非营利组织）、Blackbaud（提供 CRM 和筹款功能）和 Candid（提供补助金和慈善机会数据）。

**免费培训课程：** "非营利组织 AI 素养"课程教导员工利用 AI 进行拨款撰写、项目评估、捐助者参与和组织效率。课程不需要技术背景。

### 实施合作伙伴

Anthropic 与 The Bridgespan Group、Idealist Consulting、Vera Solutions 和 Slalom 合作，通过战略指导和组织范围的实施来支持非营利组织的采用。

### 实际应用

癫痫基金会、国际救援委员会和 IDinsight 等组织展示了可衡量的影响，包括"调查准备速度快 16 倍"和加速人道主义响应能力。
