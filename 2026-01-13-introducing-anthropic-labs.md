# 介绍 Anthropic Labs
# Introducing Labs

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-anthropic-labs
> 日期: 2026-01-13

---

## English

Anthropic announced the expansion of Labs, a team dedicated to developing experimental products at the frontier of Claude's capabilities.

The company explained that Claude's rapid evolution requires a distinctive approach: "tinkering and experimenting at the edge of what Claude can do, testing unpolished versions with early users to find what works."

Recent successes from this methodology include Claude Code, which achieved billion-dollar product status within six months of its research preview launch. The Model Context Protocol (MCP) reached 100 million monthly downloads and became an industry standard for connecting AI systems to tools and data. Additional products mentioned include Skills, Claude in Chrome, and Cowork.

### Leadership Changes

Mike Krieger, Instagram co-founder and former Anthropic Chief Product Officer, is joining Labs to work alongside Ben Mann. Ami Vora, who joined Anthropic in late 2025, will lead the Product organization, collaborating with CTO Rahul Patil to expand Claude experiences for millions of users.

### Company Vision

Daniela Amodei, President of Anthropic, stated: "The speed of advancement in AI demands a different approach to how we build, how we organize, and where we focus."

The company is actively recruiting builders experienced in creating products people value and responsibly shaping emerging technology. Interested candidates can apply through Anthropic's careers page.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 宣布扩大 Labs——一个致力于在 Claude 能力前沿开发实验性产品的团队。

公司解释说，Claude 的快速演进需要一种独特的方法："在 Claude 能做什么的边缘进行修补和实验，与早期用户测试未完善的版本以找到有效的方法。"

这种方法论的近期成功案例包括 Claude Code，它在研究预览发布后六个月内实现了十亿美元的产品地位。模型上下文协议 (MCP) 达到了每月 1 亿次下载，并成为连接 AI 系统与工具和数据的行业标准。其他提到的产品包括 Skills、Claude in Chrome 和 Cowork。

### 领导层变动

Instagram 联合创始人、前 Anthropic 首席产品官 Mike Krieger 将加入 Labs，与 Ben Mann 一起工作。2025 年末加入 Anthropic 的 Ami Vora 将领导产品组织，与 CTO Rahul Patil 合作，为数百万用户扩展 Claude 体验。

### 公司愿景

Anthropic 总裁 Daniela Amodei 表示："AI 进步的速度要求我们在构建方式、组织方式和关注重点上采取不同的方法。"

公司正在积极招聘在创造人们重视的产品和负责任地塑造新兴技术方面有经验的建设者。
