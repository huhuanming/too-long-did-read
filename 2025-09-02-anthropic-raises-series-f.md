# Anthropic 以 1830 亿美元估值完成 130 亿美元 F 轮融资
# Anthropic Raises $13B Series F at $183B Post-Money Valuation

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-raises-series-f-at-usd183b-post-money-valuation
> 日期: 2025-09-02

---

## English

Anthropic completed a $13 billion Series F funding round led by ICONIQ, valuing the company at $183 billion post-money. The round was co-led by Fidelity Management & Research Company and Lightspeed Venture Partners.

### Key Details

**Investors:** The funding included significant participation from Altimeter, Baillie Gifford, BlackRock, Blackstone, Coatue, D1 Capital Partners, General Atlantic, General Catalyst, GIC, Goldman Sachs Growth Equity, Insight Partners, Jane Street, Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan, Qatar Investment Authority, TPG, T. Rowe Price entities, WCM Investment Management, and XN.

**Revenue Growth:** Anthropic's run-rate revenue grew from approximately $1 billion at the start of 2025 to over $5 billion by August 2025—in just eight months.

**Customer Base:** The company now serves over 300,000 business customers, with large accounts (generating $100,000+ in annual revenue) growing nearly sevenfold over the past year.

### Platform Expansion

- **Enterprise:** API and industry-specific solutions for seamless AI integration
- **Developers:** Claude Code generated $500 million in run-rate revenue with 10x usage growth in three months since its May 2025 launch
- **Consumers:** Pro and Max plans offering enhanced capabilities

### Strategic Use of Funds

The investment will support expanding enterprise capacity, deepening safety research, and facilitating international expansion while continuing development of reliable, interpretable AI systems.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 完成了由 ICONIQ 领投的 130 亿美元 F 轮融资，公司投后估值达 1830 亿美元。该轮融资由 Fidelity Management & Research Company 和 Lightspeed Venture Partners 联合领投。

### 关键细节

**投资者：** 本轮融资获得了众多知名投资者的大力参与，包括 Altimeter、Baillie Gifford、BlackRock、Blackstone、Coatue、D1 Capital Partners、General Atlantic、General Catalyst、GIC、Goldman Sachs Growth Equity、Insight Partners、Jane Street、安大略教师养老金计划、卡塔尔投资局、TPG、T. Rowe Price 旗下实体、WCM Investment Management 和 XN。

**收入增长：** Anthropic 的年化收入从 2025 年初的约 10 亿美元增长到 2025 年 8 月的超过 50 亿美元——仅用了八个月。

**客户群体：** 公司目前服务超过 30 万企业客户，大客户（年收入超过 10 万美元）在过去一年增长了近七倍。

### 平台扩展

- **企业：** API 和行业特定解决方案，实现无缝 AI 集成
- **开发者：** Claude Code 自 2025 年 5 月推出以来，三个月内使用量增长 10 倍，产生了 5 亿美元的年化收入
- **消费者：** Pro 和 Max 计划提供增强功能

### 资金战略用途

本次投资将用于扩大企业服务能力、深化安全研究和推进国际化扩张，同时继续开发可靠、可解释的 AI 系统。
