# Claude 的新宪法
# Claude's New Constitution

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-new-constitution
> 日期: 2026-01-22

---

## English

Anthropic announced a new constitution for Claude, its AI model. This document serves as a foundational guide expressing and shaping Claude's values and behavior.

### Purpose and Function

The constitution is described as "a detailed description of Anthropic's vision for Claude's values and behavior" that explains the context of Claude's operation. It directly shapes Claude's behavior during training and helps Claude understand its situation and the reasoning behind how it's designed.

### Release Details

Anthropic released Claude's constitution under a Creative Commons CC0 1.0 license, allowing free use by anyone for any purpose.

### Key Philosophy

Rather than listing standalone principles, the new approach explains *why* certain behaviors matter, not just *what* is expected. The document emphasizes that AI models need to understand reasoning to generalize across novel situations, moving beyond rigid rule-following.

### Four Core Priorities

1. **Broadly safe** - protecting human oversight mechanisms during AI development
2. **Broadly ethical** - honesty, good values, avoiding harmful actions
3. **Compliant with guidelines** - following Anthropic-specific instructions
4. **Genuinely helpful** - benefiting operators and end users

### Main Constitutional Sections

- Helpfulness and user interaction
- Anthropic's supplementary guidelines
- Claude's ethical standards
- Safety and human oversight
- Claude's nature and consciousness considerations

### Technical Training Role

Claude uses the constitution to generate synthetic training data, including conversations and response rankings, supporting future model development.

### Ongoing Development

The constitution is framed as a "living document" subject to continuous refinement, with Anthropic seeking feedback from external experts in law, philosophy, theology, and psychology.

### Transparency Statement

Anthropic acknowledges gaps between intended behavior and actual outputs may exist, committing to transparency through system cards and continued research into model alignment and interpretability.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 发布了 Claude 的新宪法——这是一份表达和塑造其 AI 模型价值观和行为的基础性指导文件。

### 目的和功能

该宪法被描述为"Anthropic 对 Claude 价值观和行为的详细愿景"，解释了 Claude 运行的上下文。它直接在训练过程中塑造 Claude 的行为，帮助 Claude 理解自身的处境及其设计背后的推理。

### 发布详情

Anthropic 以知识共享 CC0 1.0 许可证发布了 Claude 的宪法，允许任何人出于任何目的免费使用。

### 核心理念

新方法不是列出独立的原则，而是解释*为什么*某些行为很重要，而不仅仅是*什么*是被期望的。该文件强调 AI 模型需要理解推理才能在新情况下泛化，超越刚性的规则遵循。

### 四个核心优先级

1. **广泛安全** - 在 AI 开发期间保护人类监督机制
2. **广泛道德** - 诚实、良好的价值观、避免有害行为
3. **遵守指南** - 遵循 Anthropic 特定的指示
4. **真正有帮助** - 使运营商和终端用户受益

### 主要宪法章节

- 有用性和用户交互
- Anthropic 的补充指南
- Claude 的道德标准
- 安全和人类监督
- Claude 的本质和意识考量

### 技术训练角色

Claude 使用宪法生成合成训练数据，包括对话和回复排名，支持未来的模型开发。

### 持续发展

该宪法被定位为"活文档"，会持续完善，Anthropic 寻求法律、哲学、神学和心理学等领域外部专家的反馈。

### 透明度声明

Anthropic 承认预期行为和实际输出之间可能存在差距，承诺通过系统卡和对模型对齐和可解释性的持续研究来保持透明度。
