# Snowflake 与 Anthropic 宣布 2 亿美元合作将智能体 AI 带给全球企业
# Snowflake and Anthropic Announce $200 Million Partnership to Bring Agentic AI to Global Enterprises

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/snowflake-anthropic-expanded-partnership
> 日期: 2025-12-03

---

## English

Today, Anthropic and Snowflake announced a significant expansion of their strategic partnership. The multi-year, $200 million agreement makes Anthropic's Claude models available in the Snowflake platform to more than 12,600 global customers across Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Azure.

Snowflake uses Claude extensively for internal operations. Claude Code enhances developer productivity and innovation across Snowflake's engineering organization. A Claude-powered GTM AI Assistant built on Snowflake Intelligence enables sales teams to centralize data, ask questions in natural language, and find answers that accelerate deal cycles.

Thousands of Snowflake customers already process trillions of Claude tokens monthly through Snowflake Cortex AI. The next partnership phase focuses on deploying AI agents capable of handling complex, multi-step analysis, powered by Claude's advanced reasoning and Snowflake's governed data environment. The system achieves "greater than 90% accuracy on complex text-to-SQL tasks."

Dario Amodei, CEO and Co-Founder of Anthropic, stated: "Enterprises have spent years building secure, trusted data environments, and now they want AI that can work within those environments without compromise."

Sridhar Ramaswamy, CEO of Snowflake, noted: "Anthropic joins a very select group of partners where we have nine-figure alignment, co-innovation at the product level, and a proven track record of executing together."

### Partnership Deliverables

- Enterprise intelligence powered by Claude Sonnet 4.5, enabling natural language queries
- Multimodal analysis across all data types through Snowflake Cortex AI Functions
- Custom multi-agent solutions via Snowflake Cortex Agents
- Built-in governance and observability through Snowflake Horizon Catalog

## 中文翻译

今天，Anthropic 和 Snowflake 宣布大幅扩展其战略合作伙伴关系。这项多年期 2 亿美元的协议使 Anthropic 的 Claude 模型可在 Snowflake 平台上通过 Amazon Bedrock、Google Cloud Vertex AI 和 Microsoft Azure 提供给超过 12,600 个全球客户。

Snowflake 广泛使用 Claude 进行内部运营。Claude Code 提高了 Snowflake 工程组织的开发者生产力和创新能力。基于 Snowflake Intelligence 构建的 Claude 驱动的 GTM AI 助手使销售团队能够集中数据、用自然语言提问并找到加速交易周期的答案。

数千 Snowflake 客户已经通过 Snowflake Cortex AI 每月处理数万亿 Claude token。下一阶段的合作专注于部署能够处理复杂多步骤分析的 AI 代理，由 Claude 的高级推理和 Snowflake 的受治理数据环境驱动。该系统在"复杂的文本到 SQL 任务上达到超过 90% 的准确率"。

Anthropic CEO 兼联合创始人 Dario Amodei 表示："企业花了多年时间构建安全、可信的数据环境，现在他们希望 AI 能够在这些环境中无妥协地工作。"

### 合作成果

- 由 Claude Sonnet 4.5 驱动的企业智能，支持自然语言查询
- 通过 Snowflake Cortex AI Functions 实现跨所有数据类型的多模态分析
- 通过 Snowflake Cortex Agents 提供自定义多代理解决方案
- 通过 Snowflake Horizon Catalog 提供内置治理和可观测性
