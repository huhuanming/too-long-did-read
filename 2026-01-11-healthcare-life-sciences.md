# 推进 Claude 在医疗保健和生命科学领域的应用
# Advancing Claude in Healthcare and the Life Sciences

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/healthcare-life-sciences
> 日期: 2026-01-11

---

## English

Anthropic announced expansions to Claude's capabilities in healthcare and life sciences sectors. The company introduced "Claude for Healthcare," offering HIPAA-ready tools for providers, payers, and health tech companies, alongside new connectors and integrations for personal health data access.

### Key Announcements

The organization launched connectors enabling Claude to access the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services Coverage Database, International Classification of Diseases codes, and the National Provider Identifier Registry. These tools support prior authorization reviews, claims management, and provider verification.

For life sciences, Anthropic connected Claude to platforms including Medidata (clinical trial solutions), ClinicalTrials.gov, bioRxiv & medRxiv (preprint servers), Open Targets, ChEMBL (drug database), and Owkin (pathology analysis).

### Performance Improvements

Claude Opus 4.5 demonstrated significant advances on medical benchmarks, including MedCalc accuracy assessments and MedAgentBench task completion evaluations. The model showed improved performance on life sciences tasks encompassing scientific figure interpretation and protein understanding.

### Personal Health Integration

US Claude Pro and Max subscribers gained access to HealthEx and Function connectors for lab results and health records. Apple Health and Android Health Connect integrations rolled out in beta, allowing users to summarize medical history and prepare appointment questions.

### Healthcare Applications

The company emphasized practical uses including streamlined prior authorization processing, claims appeal support, care coordination, and ambient scribing capabilities for clinical documentation.

Anthropic partnered with organizations including Banner Health, Novo Nordisk, Genmab, Sanofi, and Elation Health in implementing these solutions.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 宣布扩展 Claude 在医疗保健和生命科学领域的能力。公司推出了"Claude for Healthcare"，为医疗服务提供者、支付方和健康科技公司提供符合 HIPAA 标准的工具，以及用于个人健康数据访问的新连接器和集成。

### 关键公告

该组织推出了连接器，使 Claude 能够访问美国医疗保险和医疗补助服务中心覆盖数据库、国际疾病分类代码和国家提供者标识符注册表。这些工具支持事先授权审查、理赔管理和提供者验证。

在生命科学方面，Anthropic 将 Claude 连接到包括 Medidata（临床试验解决方案）、ClinicalTrials.gov、bioRxiv 和 medRxiv（预印本服务器）、Open Targets、ChEMBL（药物数据库）和 Owkin（病理分析）在内的平台。

### 性能改进

Claude Opus 4.5 在医学基准测试中展示了显著进步，包括 MedCalc 准确性评估和 MedAgentBench 任务完成评估。该模型在包括科学图形解释和蛋白质理解在内的生命科学任务上显示出改进的性能。

### 个人健康集成

美国 Claude Pro 和 Max 订阅者获得了 HealthEx 和 Function 连接器的访问权限，用于实验室结果和健康记录。Apple Health 和 Android Health Connect 集成以测试版推出，允许用户总结病史和准备就诊问题。

### 医疗保健应用

公司强调了实际用途，包括简化事先授权处理、理赔申诉支持、护理协调和临床文档的环境转录能力。

Anthropic 与 Banner Health、Novo Nordisk、Genmab、Sanofi 和 Elation Health 等组织合作实施这些解决方案。
