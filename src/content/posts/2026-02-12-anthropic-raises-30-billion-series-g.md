## English

Anthropic has secured $30 billion in Series G funding, with the round led by GIC and Coatue. This valuation places the company at $380 billion post-money. Co-leaders include D. E. Shaw Ventures, Dragoneer, Founders Fund, ICONIQ, and MGX.

The funding round features significant participation from: Accel, Addition, Alpha Wave Global, Altimeter, AMP PBC, Appaloosa LP, Baillie Gifford, Bessemer Venture Partners, BlackRock affiliated funds, Blackstone, D1 Capital Partners, Fidelity Management & Research Company, General Catalyst, Greenoaks, Goldman Sachs Growth Equity, Insight Partners, Jane Street, JPMorganChase, Lightspeed Venture Partners, Menlo Ventures, Morgan Stanley Investment Management, NX1 Capital, Qatar Investment Authority, Sands Capital, Sequoia Capital, Temasek, TowerBrook, TPG, Whale Rock Capital, and XN. Microsoft and NVIDIA contributions from previous announcements are included.

Krishna Rao, CFO, stated: "Claude is increasingly becoming critical to how businesses work" and highlighted "incredible demand" from customers spanning entrepreneurs to enterprises.

The company reached $14 billion in run-rate revenue, having achieved this milestone in under three years from initial revenue generation. The business has demonstrated 10x annual growth across the past three years.

Claude Code, launched publicly in May 2025, now generates over $2.5 billion in run-rate revenue—doubling since early 2026. Weekly active users have doubled since January 1. Analysis indicates Claude Code authors approximately 4% of GitHub public commits globally, up from 2% one month prior.

Business subscriptions to Claude Code have quadrupled since 2026's start, with enterprise usage representing over half of total revenue. The platform enables work in financial analysis, sales, cybersecurity, and scientific discovery.

January alone saw over thirty product and feature launches, including Cowork, which extends Claude Code capabilities across knowledge work. Cowork provides eleven open-source plugins for specialization in sales, legal, and finance. Healthcare and life sciences reached HIPAA compliance.

Claude's latest model, Opus 4.6, manages entire work categories, generating professional documents, spreadsheets, and presentations. It leads on GDPval-AA, measuring performance on economically valuable knowledge tasks.

The Series G funds infrastructure expansion. Claude remains available across Amazon Web Services (Bedrock), Google Cloud (Vertex AI), and Microsoft Azure (Foundry). The company trains on diversified hardware including AWS Trainium, Google TPUs, and NVIDIA GPUs for optimal workload matching and enterprise resilience.

The company serves eight Fortune 10 companies. Customer spending above $100,000 annually grew 7x year-over-year. Over 500 customers now spend $1 million annually, compared to twelve two years ago.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 已完成 300 亿美元的 G 轮融资，该轮由 GIC 和 Coatue 领投。这一估值使公司的投后估值达到 3800 亿美元。联合领投方包括 D. E. Shaw Ventures、Dragoneer、Founders Fund、ICONIQ 和 MGX。

本轮融资获得了大量参与，包括：Accel、Addition、Alpha Wave Global、Altimeter、AMP PBC、Appaloosa LP、Baillie Gifford、Bessemer Venture Partners、贝莱德关联基金、黑石、D1 Capital Partners、Fidelity Management & Research Company、General Catalyst、Greenoaks、高盛成长股权、Insight Partners、Jane Street、摩根大通、Lightspeed Venture Partners、Menlo Ventures、摩根士丹利投资管理、NX1 Capital、卡塔尔投资局、Sands Capital、红杉资本、淡马锡、TowerBrook、TPG、Whale Rock Capital 和 XN。此前宣布的微软和英伟达的贡献也包含在内。

CFO Krishna Rao 表示："Claude 正日益成为企业工作方式的关键"，并强调了从创业者到企业客户的"令人难以置信的需求"。

公司的运营率收入达到 140 亿美元，从最初产生收入到达到这一里程碑不到三年。业务在过去三年展示了 10 倍的年度增长。

Claude Code 于 2025 年 5 月公开发布，目前产生超过 25 亿美元的运营率收入——自 2026 年初以来翻了一番。每周活跃用户自 1 月 1 日以来翻了一番。分析表明 Claude Code 在全球范围内贡献了约 4% 的 GitHub 公共提交，一个月前这一比例为 2%。

自 2026 年初以来，Claude Code 的商业订阅增长了四倍，企业使用量占总收入的一半以上。该平台支持金融分析、销售、网络安全和科学发现等领域的工作。

仅 1 月份就推出了三十多个产品和功能，包括 Cowork，它将 Claude Code 的能力扩展到知识工作领域。Cowork 提供了十一个开源插件，专注于销售、法律和金融领域。医疗保健和生命科学达到了 HIPAA 合规性。

Claude 的最新模型 Opus 4.6 可以管理整个工作类别，生成专业文档、电子表格和演示文稿。它在 GDPval-AA 上领先，该基准衡量经济价值知识任务的性能。

G 轮资金用于基础设施扩展。Claude 仍可通过 Amazon Web Services (Bedrock)、Google Cloud (Vertex AI) 和 Microsoft Azure (Foundry) 获得。公司在多样化硬件上进行训练，包括 AWS Trainium、Google TPU 和 NVIDIA GPU，以实现最佳工作负载匹配和企业弹性。

公司服务于八家财富 10 强企业。年支出超过 10 万美元的客户同比增长 7 倍。目前有超过 500 个客户年支出达到 100 万美元，而两年前只有 12 个。
