## English

Anthropic and Infosys, a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting founded and headquartered in Bengaluru, announced a collaboration to develop and deliver enterprise AI solutions across telecommunications, financial services, manufacturing, and software development.

The collaboration integrates Anthropic's Claude models and Claude Code with Infosys Topaz, an AI-first set of services, solutions, and platforms using generative and agentic AI technologies, to help companies speed up software development and adopt AI with the governance and transparency that regulated industries require.

India is the second-largest market for Claude.ai, home to a developer community doing some of the most technically intense AI work observed anywhere: nearly half of Claude usage in India involves building applications, modernizing systems, and shipping production software. Infosys is one of the first partners in Anthropic's expanded presence in India.

Dario Amodei, CEO and Co-founder of Anthropic, stated: "There's a big gap between an AI model that works in a demo and one that works in a regulated industry—and if you want to close that gap, you need domain expertise. Infosys has exactly that kind of expertise."

Salil Parekh, Chief Executive Officer of Infosys, noted: "AI is not just transforming business, it is redefining the way industries operate and innovate. Our collaboration with Anthropic marks a strategic leap toward advancing enterprise AI."

### Building Agentic AI for Regulated Industries

A core focus involves agentic AI systems that go beyond answering questions to independently handling multi-step tasks like processing claims, generating and testing code, or managing compliance reviews. Using tools like the Claude Agent SDK, both companies will help clients build AI agents that work persistently across long, complex tasks and processes. The collaboration will also help organizations modernize legacy systems, combining Infosys Topaz and Claude to make migration faster and reduce costs.

**Industry-Specific Applications:**

- **Telecommunications:** AI agents will modernize network operations, simplify customer lifecycle management, and improve service delivery in this operationally complex and regulated sector.

- **Financial Services:** AI agents will detect and assess risk faster, automate compliance reporting, and deliver personalized customer interactions.

- **Manufacturing and Engineering:** Claude will accelerate product design and simulation, reducing R&D timelines and enabling more iterations before production.

- **Software Development:** Teams will use Claude Code to write, test, and debug code, helping developers move faster from design to production.

- **Enterprise Operations:** Claude Cowork will automate routine work like document summarization, status reporting, and review cycles.

Visit the Enterprise page to learn more about Claude. Claude is the only frontier model available on all three of the world's most prominent cloud services: Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Azure.

### 为受监管行业构建智能体 AI

核心重点是智能体 AI 系统，它们不仅仅是回答问题，而是独立处理多步骤任务，如处理理赔、生成和测试代码，或管理合规审查。使用 Claude Agent SDK 等工具，两家公司将帮助客户构建能够在长期复杂任务和流程中持续工作的 AI 智能体。此次合作还将帮助组织现代化遗留系统，结合 Infosys Topaz 和 Claude 使迁移更快并降低成本。

**行业特定应用：**

- **电信：** AI 智能体将现代化网络运营，简化客户生命周期管理，并改善这个运营复杂且受监管行业的服务交付。

- **金融服务：** AI 智能体将更快地检测和评估风险，自动化合规报告，并提供个性化的客户互动。

- **制造和工程：** Claude 将加速产品设计和仿真，缩短研发时间线并在生产前实现更多迭代。

- **软件开发：** 团队将使用 Claude Code 编写、测试和调试代码，帮助开发人员更快地从设计到生产。

- **企业运营：** Claude Cowork 将自动化文档摘要、状态报告和审查周期等日常工作。
