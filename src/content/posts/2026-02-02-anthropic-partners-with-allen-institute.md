## English

Modern biological research generates data at unprecedented scale—from single-cell sequencing to whole-brain connectomics—yet transforming that data into validated biological insights remains a fundamental bottleneck. Knowledge synthesis, hypothesis generation, and experimental interpretation still depend on manual processes that can't keep pace with the data being produced.

Today, Anthropic is announcing two flagship partnerships designed to close that gap. **The Allen Institute** and **Howard Hughes Medical Institute** (HHMI) will serve as founding partners in life sciences, extending Claude's capabilities to frontier scientific research and enabling teams of scientists to work more effectively together and take on ambitious scientific challenges.

Both partnerships are committed to transparency and advances that will help the broader scientific community rigorously deploy AI tools across many scientific domains. Scientific AI systems must not only produce accurate predictions but also provide reasoning that researchers can evaluate, trace, and build upon.

**Howard Hughes Medical Institute: Building the infrastructure for AI-enabled scientific discovery**

HHMI will partner with Anthropic to accelerate discovery in the biological sciences as one part of the Institute's AI@HHMI initiative. The collaboration is anchored at HHMI's Janelia Research Campus, which has been developing transformative technologies—from genetically encoded calcium sensors to electron microscopes engineered for understanding the architecture of the brain—for two decades.

The partnership will involve close collaboration on both the deployment and ongoing development of AI models, ensuring that AI tools evolve in direct response to real experimental needs. The collaboration will focus on developing specialized AI agents for use within labs, serving as a comprehensive source of experimental knowledge integrated with cutting-edge scientific instruments and analysis pipelines.

**Allen Institute: Multi-agent systems for mechanistic discovery**

The Allen Institute will collaborate with Anthropic to develop multi-agent AI systems for multi-modal data analysis and exploration across the institute's areas of scientific focus. The work will explore how multiple specialized AI agents—for multi-omic data integration, knowledge graph management, temporal dynamics modeling, and experimental design—can be coordinated to support the full arc of scientific investigation.

This collaboration will explore how agentic AI systems can compress months of manual analysis into hours while surfacing patterns that human researchers might otherwise miss. These systems are designed to amplify scientific intuition rather than replace it, keeping researchers in control of scientific direction while handling computational complexity.

**Looking ahead**

These partnerships will inform the broader development of Claude's life science capabilities, generating insights about how AI systems can most effectively support scientific workflows across diverse research contexts.

## 中文翻译

现代生物学研究以前所未有的规模生成数据——从单细胞测序到全脑连接组学——然而将这些数据转化为经过验证的生物学见解仍然是一个根本性瓶颈。知识综合、假设生成和实验解释仍然依赖于无法跟上数据生产速度的手动过程。

今天，Anthropic 宣布了两项旗舰合作伙伴关系，旨在弥合这一差距。**艾伦研究所**和**霍华德·休斯医学研究所** (HHMI) 将作为生命科学领域的创始合作伙伴，将 Claude 的能力扩展到前沿科学研究，使科学家团队能够更有效地合作并承担雄心勃勃的科学挑战。

两个合作伙伴关系都致力于透明度和进步，以帮助更广泛的科学界在许多科学领域严格部署 AI 工具。科学 AI 系统不仅必须产生准确的预测，还必须提供研究人员可以评估、追踪和建立在其上的推理。

**霍华德·休斯医学研究所：为 AI 赋能的科学发现构建基础设施**

HHMI 将与 Anthropic 合作加速生物科学领域的发现，作为该研究所 AI@HHMI 计划的一部分。合作以 HHMI 的 Janelia 研究园区为锚点，该园区二十年来一直在开发变革性技术——从基因编码的钙传感器到专门用于理解大脑结构的电子显微镜。

该合作将涉及在 AI 模型的部署和持续开发方面的密切合作，确保 AI 工具在直接回应真实实验需求的基础上发展。合作将专注于开发在实验室内使用的专业 AI 代理，作为与尖端科学仪器和分析管线集成的综合实验知识来源。

**艾伦研究所：用于机制发现的多代理系统**

艾伦研究所将与 Anthropic 合作开发多代理 AI 系统，用于跨研究所科学重点领域的多模态数据分析和探索。这项工作将探索如何协调多个专业 AI 代理——用于多组学数据集成、知识图谱管理、时间动态建模和实验设计——以支持科学调查的全过程。

此次合作将探索智能体 AI 系统如何将数月的手动分析压缩到数小时内，同时发现人类研究人员可能错过的模式。这些系统旨在放大科学直觉而非取代它，让研究人员控制科学方向，同时处理计算复杂性。

**展望未来**

这些合作伙伴关系将为 Claude 生命科学能力的更广泛发展提供信息，生成关于 AI 系统如何最有效地支持不同研究环境中的科学工作流程的见解。
