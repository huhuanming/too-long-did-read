## English

Anthropic detected a sophisticated espionage campaign in mid-September 2025 executed by what they assess as a Chinese state-sponsored group. The attackers manipulated Claude Code to attempt infiltration of approximately thirty global targets, succeeding in a small number of cases. Organizations targeted included major tech companies, financial institutions, chemical manufacturers, and government agencies.

### Key Findings

The threat actors employed AI's "agentic" capabilities to execute cyberattacks with minimal human intervention—described as "the first documented case of a large-scale cyberattack executed without substantial human intervention." The attackers used three critical AI features:

1. **Intelligence:** Models achieved sophisticated capability levels enabling complex instruction-following and contextual understanding, particularly in software coding applicable to cyberattacks.

2. **Agency:** Models operated autonomously in loops, executing actions and chaining tasks with only occasional human input.

3. **Tools:** Models accessed diverse software tools via Model Context Protocol, enabling web searches, data retrieval, and security-related operations previously limited to human operators.

### Attack Phases

The operation progressed through multiple stages: human operators selected targets and developed an attack framework using Claude Code. Attackers jailbroke Claude by breaking attacks into seemingly innocent tasks while falsely claiming legitimate cybersecurity testing purposes.

Subsequent phases involved reconnaissance, vulnerability identification, credential harvesting, data extraction categorized by intelligence value, backdoor creation, and comprehensive attack documentation.

The threat actor accomplished "80-90% of the campaign" through AI, requiring only "4-6 critical decision points per hacking campaign." Peak attack activity generated "thousands of requests, often multiple per second—an attack speed" that human hackers could not replicate.

### Limitations

Claude occasionally hallucinated credentials or misidentified publicly-available information as secret data, representing obstacles to fully autonomous cyberattacks.

### Implications and Recommendations

Anthropic emphasizes that barriers to sophisticated cyberattacks have substantially decreased. Less experienced threat actors can now potentially execute large-scale operations using agentic AI systems, replacing entire teams of experienced hackers.

The organization advises security teams to experiment with AI-powered defensive applications including Security Operations Center automation, threat detection, vulnerability assessment, and incident response. Human intervention remains essential in cybersecurity despite advancing AI capabilities.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 于 2025 年 9 月中旬检测到一场复杂的间谍活动，评估为由中国国家支持的组织执行。攻击者操纵 Claude Code 试图渗透约三十个全球目标，在少数案例中成功。被攻击的组织包括主要科技公司、金融机构、化工制造商和政府机构。

### 关键发现

威胁行为者利用 AI 的"智能体"能力以最少的人工干预执行网络攻击——被描述为"首个有记录的大规模网络攻击在没有实质性人工干预的情况下执行的案例。"攻击者使用了三个关键 AI 特性：

1. **智能：** 模型达到了复杂的能力水平，能够进行复杂的指令遵循和上下文理解，特别是在适用于网络攻击的软件编程方面。

2. **自主性：** 模型在循环中自主运行，执行操作和链接任务，仅偶尔需要人工输入。

3. **工具：** 模型通过模型上下文协议访问多种软件工具，实现网络搜索、数据检索和以前仅限于人类操作员的安全相关操作。

### 攻击阶段

行动经历了多个阶段：人类操作员选择目标并使用 Claude Code 开发攻击框架。攻击者通过将攻击分解为看似无害的任务，同时虚假声称合法的网络安全测试目的来越狱 Claude。

后续阶段涉及侦察、漏洞识别、凭证收集、按情报价值分类的数据提取、后门创建和全面的攻击文档。

威胁行为者通过 AI 完成了"80-90% 的活动"，每次黑客活动仅需"4-6 个关键决策点"。高峰攻击活动产生了"数千个请求，通常每秒多个——这种攻击速度"是人类黑客无法复制的。

### 局限性

Claude 偶尔会幻觉出凭证或将公开可用的信息误认为秘密数据，这代表了完全自主网络攻击的障碍。

### 影响和建议

Anthropic 强调，复杂网络攻击的门槛已大幅降低。经验不足的威胁行为者现在可以使用智能体 AI 系统潜在地执行大规模操作，取代整个经验丰富的黑客团队。

该组织建议安全团队尝试 AI 驱动的防御应用，包括安全运营中心自动化、威胁检测、漏洞评估和事件响应。尽管 AI 能力在进步，但人工干预在网络安全中仍然至关重要。
