## English

Anthropic is partnering with CodePath, the nation's largest provider of collegiate computer science education, to redesign its coding curriculum as AI reshapes the field of software development. CodePath will put Claude and Claude Code at the center of its courses and career programs, giving more than 20,000 students at community colleges, state schools, and HBCUs access to frontier AI tools as part of their education.

Over 40% of CodePath students come from families earning under $50,000 a year, and CodePath aims to provide them with industry-vetted courses and access to career networks traditionally reserved for students at wealthier institutions.

### What We're Building

CodePath is integrating Claude into its AI courses—including Foundations of AI Engineering, Applications of AI Engineering, and AI Open-Source Capstone—so students can learn to build with tools like Claude Code and contribute to real-world open-source projects.

In the fall of 2025, over 100 CodePath students piloted Claude Code to contribute to open-source projects like GitLab, Puter, and Dokploy. Students said they found the experience both valuable and challenging. "Claude Code was instrumental in my learning process" said Laney Hood, CodePath student and computer science major at Texas Tech University, who entered with limited experience in TypeScript and Node.js.

In January, Howard University announced a redesigned Intro to Artificial Intelligence course, developed in partnership with CodePath and the Thurgood Marshall College Fund. The course gives students experience with Claude-assisted software development, preparing them for entry-level engineering roles. It represents the first time CodePath's applied AI curriculum is being offered for academic credit at a university.

"We now have the technology to teach in two years what used to take four" said Michael Ellison, CodePath Co-founder and CEO, emphasizing that speed without equity widens inequality. The partnership enables students to "learn to build with Claude from day one, at institutions that have historically been overlooked."

Beyond the classroom, Anthropic and CodePath will collaborate on public research exploring how AI is changing coding education and economic opportunity dynamics.

### A Broader Commitment to AI Education

This partnership builds on Anthropic's work to equip educators and students with AI tools. Anthropic is partnering with the American Federation of Teachers to offer free AI training to their 1.8 million members across the US. In Iceland, the organization launched one of the world's first national AI education pilots with the Ministry of Education and Children. In Rwanda, they're working with the government and ALX to bring Claude-powered learning to hundreds of thousands of students and young professionals across Africa.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 正与 CodePath——美国最大的大学计算机科学教育提供商——合作，在 AI 重塑软件开发领域之际重新设计其编程课程。CodePath 将把 Claude 和 Claude Code 置于其课程和职业项目的核心，让社区学院、州立学校和历史性黑人大学 (HBCU) 的 2 万多名学生在教育过程中获得前沿 AI 工具的使用权限。

超过 40% 的 CodePath 学生来自年收入低于 5 万美元的家庭，CodePath 旨在为他们提供经过行业验证的课程和传统上仅限于富裕院校学生才能获得的职业网络。

### 我们正在构建什么

CodePath 正将 Claude 集成到其 AI 课程中——包括 AI 工程基础、AI 工程应用和 AI 开源毕业设计——以便学生能够学习使用 Claude Code 等工具进行构建并为真实世界的开源项目做贡献。

2025 年秋季，100 多名 CodePath 学生试用了 Claude Code，为 GitLab、Puter 和 Dokploy 等开源项目做贡献。学生们表示这段经历既有价值又具有挑战性。德克萨斯理工大学计算机科学专业的 CodePath 学生 Laney Hood 说："Claude Code 在我的学习过程中发挥了重要作用"，她在入学时对 TypeScript 和 Node.js 的经验有限。

1 月，霍华德大学宣布了一门重新设计的人工智能入门课程，与 CodePath 和瑟古德·马歇尔大学基金合作开发。该课程让学生获得 Claude 辅助软件开发的经验，为他们进入初级工程师岗位做准备。这是 CodePath 的应用 AI 课程首次在大学获得学分认可。

CodePath 联合创始人兼 CEO Michael Ellison 说："我们现在有技术可以在两年内教授过去需要四年的内容"，他强调没有公平的速度会加剧不平等。该合作使学生能够"从第一天起就学习使用 Claude 进行构建，在那些历来被忽视的机构中。"

除课堂外，Anthropic 和 CodePath 还将合作开展公共研究，探索 AI 如何改变编程教育和经济机会的动态。

### 对 AI 教育的更广泛承诺

这一合作伙伴关系建立在 Anthropic 为教育工作者和学生配备 AI 工具的工作基础上。Anthropic 正与美国教师联合会合作，为其在美国各地的 180 万会员提供免费 AI 培训。在冰岛，该组织与教育和儿童部共同启动了世界上首批国家级 AI 教育试点之一。在卢旺达，他们正与政府和 ALX 合作，将 Claude 驱动的学习带给非洲数十万学生和年轻专业人士。
