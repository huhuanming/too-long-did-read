## English

Anthropic and the US Department of Energy (DOE) are announcing a multi-year partnership as part of the Genesis Mission—the Department's initiative to use AI to cement America's leadership in science. The partnership focuses on three domains: American energy dominance, biological and life sciences, and scientific productivity. It has potential to affect work at all 17 of America's national laboratories.

The Genesis Mission recognizes a critical moment where global AI competition intensifies. America must harness its scientific infrastructure—from supercomputers to decades of experimental data—combined with frontier AI capabilities to maintain scientific leadership.

Jared Kaplan, Anthropic's Chief Science Officer, stated: "Anthropic was founded by scientists who believe AI can deliver transformative progress for research itself. The Genesis Mission is the sort of ambitious, rigorous program where that belief gets tested."

### The Partnership

Anthropic seeks to provide DOE researchers access to Claude and Anthropic engineering teams who will develop purpose-built tools including:

- AI "agents" for DOE's highest-priority challenges
- Model Context Protocol servers connecting Claude to scientific instruments and tools
- Claude Skills for specialized expertise on relevant scientific workflows

Claude can facilitate advancements in:

- **Energy dominance:** Speeding up permitting review processes, advancing nuclear technology research, and strengthening domestic energy security
- **Biological and life sciences:** Supporting early-warning systems for pandemics, biological threat detection, and accelerating drug discovery
- **Scientific productivity:** Accessing fifty years of DOE research to accelerate research cycles and identify patterns humans might miss

### Commitment to Partner with the US Government

Scientific progress drives America's prosperity and security. Past projects include co-development of a nuclear risk classifier with the National Nuclear Security Administration and Claude deployment at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 和美国能源部 (DOE) 宣布一项多年合作伙伴关系，作为创世纪任务的一部分——该部门利用 AI 巩固美国科学领导地位的倡议。合作专注于三个领域：美国能源主导地位、生物和生命科学以及科学生产力。它有可能影响美国所有 17 个国家实验室的工作。

创世纪任务认识到全球 AI 竞争加剧的关键时刻。美国必须利用其科学基础设施——从超级计算机到数十年的实验数据——结合前沿 AI 能力来维持科学领导地位。

Anthropic 首席科学官 Jared Kaplan 表示："Anthropic 由相信 AI 能为研究本身带来变革性进步的科学家创立。创世纪任务正是这种信念得到检验的那种雄心勃勃、严格的项目。"

### 合作内容

Anthropic 寻求为 DOE 研究人员提供 Claude 的访问权限和 Anthropic 工程团队，他们将开发专用工具，包括：

- 用于 DOE 最高优先级挑战的 AI "智能体"
- 将 Claude 连接到科学仪器和工具的模型上下文协议服务器
- 用于相关科学工作流专业知识的 Claude Skills

Claude 可以促进以下方面的进步：

- **能源主导：** 加速许可审查流程，推进核技术研究，增强国内能源安全
- **生物和生命科学：** 支持大流行病早期预警系统、生物威胁检测和加速药物发现
- **科学生产力：** 访问 DOE 五十年的研究以加速研究周期并识别人类可能遗漏的模式

### 与美国政府合作的承诺

科学进步推动美国的繁荣和安全。过去的项目包括与国家核安全管理局共同开发核风险分类器，以及在劳伦斯利弗莫尔国家实验室部署 Claude。
