# Anthropic 开设班加罗尔办公室并宣布印度新合作伙伴关系
# Anthropic Opens Bengaluru Office and Announces New Partnerships Across India

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/bengaluru-office-partnerships-across-india
> 日期: 2026-02-16

---

## English

India represents Anthropic's second-largest market for Claude.ai, with a developer community engaged in technically demanding AI work. The company notes that "nearly half of Claude usage in India comprises computer and mathematical tasks: building applications, modernizing systems, and shipping production software."

Today, Anthropic officially inaugurated its Bengaluru office, announcing partnerships spanning enterprise, education, and agriculture sectors. Irina Ghose, Managing Director of India, stated: "India represents one of the world's most promising opportunities to bring the benefits of responsible AI to vastly more people and enterprises."

### Building Language Capabilities for a Billion Speakers

Anthropic launched a company-wide initiative six months prior to enhance AI model performance across ten widely spoken Indian languages: Hindi, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu, Tamil, Punjabi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, and Urdu. The company collaborated with Karya and the Collective Intelligence Project to develop evaluations testing performance on locally relevant tasks in agriculture and law, partnering with Digital Green and Adalat AI.

### Partnering with Enterprises, Digital Natives, and Startups

Run-rate revenue in India has doubled since October 2025's expansion announcement. The company supports enterprises and startups through applied AI expertise for designing and scaling Claude-powered solutions.

Notable implementations include Air India using Claude Code for faster software development, CRED achieving 2x faster feature delivery and 10% better test coverage, and Cognizant deploying Claude to 350,000 global employees for legacy system modernization.

Startup successes feature prominently: Razorpay integrates AI into risk systems and operations, Rocket enables non-technical teams to build production applications rapidly, Enterpret powers its AI assistant with Claude, and Emergent achieved $25 million annual recurring revenue with two million users in under five months using Claude exclusively.

### Reaching Students in Low-Income Communities

Educational tasks comprise 12% of Claude.ai usage in India. Pratham, a major education nonprofit, partnered with Anthropic as its first strategic AI lab partner. The Anytime Testing Machine, powered by Claude, currently pilots with 1,500 students across twenty schools, with expansion to 100 schools planned by year-end 2026.

Anthropic collaborates with Central Square Foundation to develop AI-enabled tools including personalized tutors and assessment-driven instruction targeting underserved primary school students.

### Incorporating AI into the Public Sector

India's agriculture sector comprises nearly one-sixth of the economy and employs approximately half the labor force. Anthropic partners with EkStep Foundation to expand expert knowledge access via Claude deployments through the OpenAgriNet initiative.

Addressing India's 50 million pending court cases, Adalat AI launched a national WhatsApp helpline today. The service provides instant case updates, translation, document summarization, and interactive legal document querying in Indian languages using Claude.

### Driving Adoption Through Open-Source Standards

India's Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, supported by Bharat Digital, launched the first official Indian government MCP server, enabling authoritative national statistics access. Swiggy utilizes MCP to permit grocery ordering and dining reservations through Claude.

### Growing Anthropic's Presence in India

The Bengaluru office—Anthropic's second Asian location following Tokyo—officially opened under Ghose's leadership. The office prioritizes hiring local talent across multiple roles.

## 中文翻译

印度是 Anthropic Claude.ai 的第二大市场，拥有一个从事技术要求高的 AI 工作的开发者社区。公司指出"印度近一半的 Claude 使用涉及计算机和数学任务：构建应用程序、现代化系统和发布生产软件。"

今天，Anthropic 正式启用了班加罗尔办公室，宣布了涵盖企业、教育和农业领域的合作伙伴关系。印度董事总经理 Irina Ghose 表示："印度代表着世界上最有前景的机会之一，可以将负责任的 AI 的好处带给更多的人和企业。"

### 为十亿讲者构建语言能力

Anthropic 在六个月前启动了一项全公司范围的计划，以提升 AI 模型在十种广泛使用的印度语言中的性能：印地语、孟加拉语、马拉地语、泰卢固语、泰米尔语、旁遮普语、古吉拉特语、卡纳达语、马拉雅拉姆语和乌尔都语。公司与 Karya 和集体智能项目合作开发评估，测试在农业和法律等本地相关任务上的性能，并与 Digital Green 和 Adalat AI 合作。

### 与企业、数字原住民和初创公司合作

自 2025 年 10 月宣布扩张以来，印度的运营率收入翻了一番。公司通过应用 AI 专业知识支持企业和初创公司设计和扩展基于 Claude 的解决方案。

值得注意的实施包括：Air India 使用 Claude Code 加速软件开发，CRED 实现了 2 倍的功能交付速度和 10% 的测试覆盖率提升，Cognizant 向 35 万全球员工部署 Claude 进行遗留系统现代化。

初创公司的成功尤为突出：Razorpay 将 AI 集成到风险系统和运营中，Rocket 使非技术团队能够快速构建生产应用，Enterpret 使用 Claude 为其 AI 助手提供动力，Emergent 在不到五个月内使用 Claude 实现了 2500 万美元的年度经常性收入和 200 万用户。

### 触达低收入社区的学生

教育任务占印度 Claude.ai 使用量的 12%。主要教育非营利组织 Pratham 作为其首个战略 AI 实验室合作伙伴与 Anthropic 合作。由 Claude 驱动的随时测试机器目前在二十所学校的 1500 名学生中试点，计划到 2026 年底扩展到 100 所学校。

Anthropic 与 Central Square Foundation 合作开发 AI 工具，包括针对服务不足的小学生的个性化辅导和评估驱动教学。

### 将 AI 融入公共部门

印度农业部门约占经济的六分之一，雇用了约一半的劳动力。Anthropic 与 EkStep Foundation 合作，通过 OpenAgriNet 计划通过 Claude 部署扩大专家知识访问。

针对印度 5000 万积压的法院案件，Adalat AI 今天推出了全国 WhatsApp 帮助热线。该服务使用 Claude 以印度语言提供即时案件更新、翻译、文档摘要和交互式法律文档查询。

### 通过开源标准推动采用

印度统计和计划实施部在 Bharat Digital 的支持下推出了首个印度官方政府 MCP 服务器，实现了权威国家统计数据的访问。Swiggy 利用 MCP 允许通过 Claude 进行杂货订购和餐厅预订。

### 扩大 Anthropic 在印度的业务

班加罗尔办公室——继东京之后 Anthropic 的第二个亚洲办公地点——在 Ghose 的领导下正式开放。办公室优先招聘多个职位的本地人才。
