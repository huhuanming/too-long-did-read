# Anthropic Appoints Irina Ghose as Managing Director of India
# Anthropic 任命 Irina Ghose 为印度董事总经理

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-appoints-irina-ghose-as-managing-director-of-india
> Date: 2026-01-16

---

Anthropic has named Irina Ghose as Managing Director of India ahead of opening its first office in the country. Ghose brings over thirty years of experience scaling technology businesses, most recently serving as Managing Director at Microsoft India.

Anthropic 在该国开设首个办公室之前，任命 Irina Ghose 为印度董事总经理。Ghose 拥有超过三十年的技术业务拓展经验，最近曾担任微软印度董事总经理。

In her previous role, Ghose led enterprise AI adoption across Indian industries including banking, healthcare, manufacturing, and government sectors. She has built high-impact teams and ecosystem partnerships while focusing on technology's potential to drive meaningful business and societal impact.

在她之前的职位上，Ghose 领导了印度银行、医疗保健、制造和政府等行业的企业 AI 采用。她在专注于技术推动有意义的商业和社会影响的潜力的同时，建立了高影响力的团队和生态系统合作伙伴关系。

---

## Key Quotes
## 重要引述

Ghose stated: "Indian organizations are moving beyond experimentation toward applied AI, where trust, safety, and long-term impact matter as much as innovation."

Ghose 表示："印度的组织正在从实验阶段迈向应用 AI，在这里，信任、安全和长期影响与创新同样重要。"

Chris Ciauri, Managing Director of International at Anthropic, commented: "Irina's expertise in scaling technology businesses makes her the ideal leader as we expand."

Anthropic 国际业务董事总经理 Chris Ciauri 评论道："Irina 在拓展技术业务方面的专长使她成为我们扩张时的理想领导者。"

---

## Strategic Focus
## 战略重点

The India team will prioritize engagement with policymakers, academic institutions, developers, and enterprises using AI to address local challenges.

印度团队将优先与政策制定者、学术机构、开发者和企业合作，利用 AI 解决当地挑战。

---

## Market Position
## 市场地位

India ranks as Claude.ai's second-largest market globally. Anthropic's fourth Economic Index revealed that Indian users concentrate nearly half their Claude.ai usage on computer and mathematical applications, demonstrating strong technical focus.

印度是 Claude.ai 在全球的第二大市场。Anthropic 的第四份经济指数报告显示，印度用户将近一半的 Claude.ai 使用集中在计算机和数学应用上，展示了强烈的技术关注。
