# Anthropic Partners with CodePath to Bring Claude to the US's Largest Collegiate Computer Science Program
# Anthropic 与 CodePath 合作，将 Claude 引入美国最大的大学计算机科学项目

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-codepath-partnership
> Date: 2026-02-13

---

Anthropic is collaborating with CodePath, the nation's premier provider of collegiate computer science education, to redesign coding curricula as artificial intelligence transforms software development. Through this partnership, Claude and Claude Code will be integrated into CodePath's courses and career programs, providing over 20,000 students at community colleges, state universities, and historically Black colleges and universities access to frontier AI capabilities.

Anthropic 正在与美国首屈一指的大学计算机科学教育提供商 CodePath 合作，在人工智能变革软件开发之际重新设计编程课程。通过此次合作，Claude 和 Claude Code 将被整合到 CodePath 的课程和职业项目中，为社区学院、州立大学和传统黑人大学的超过 20,000 名学生提供前沿 AI 能力的使用机会。

---

## Educational Equity
## 教育公平

The partnership addresses educational equity: "Over 40% of CodePath students come from families earning under $50,000 a year," and the organization aims to democratize access to industry-vetted courses and career networks typically available only at well-funded institutions.

该合作关注教育公平："超过 40% 的 CodePath 学生来自年收入低于 50,000 美元的家庭，"该组织旨在让通常只有资金充足的机构才能获得的行业认可课程和职业网络实现民主化。

---

## What's Being Built
## 正在构建的内容

CodePath is embedding Claude into three AI courses: Foundations of AI Engineering, Applications of AI Engineering, and AI Open-Source Capstone. Students will learn to build with Claude Code and contribute to real-world open-source initiatives.

CodePath 正在将 Claude 嵌入三门 AI 课程：AI 工程基础、AI 工程应用和 AI 开源毕业设计。学生将学习使用 Claude Code 进行构建并为实际的开源项目做出贡献。

During fall 2025, over 100 CodePath students piloted Claude Code on open-source projects including GitLab, Puter, and Dokploy. One participant noted the tool was "instrumental in my learning process," particularly when working with unfamiliar programming languages.

2025 年秋季，超过 100 名 CodePath 学生在包括 GitLab、Puter 和 Dokploy 在内的开源项目上试用了 Claude Code。一位参与者指出，该工具"在我的学习过程中起到了重要作用"，尤其是在使用不熟悉的编程语言时。

Howard University announced a redesigned Intro to Artificial Intelligence course developed with CodePath and the Thurgood Marshall College Fund, marking the first time CodePath's applied AI curriculum receives academic credit.

霍华德大学宣布了与 CodePath 和 Thurgood Marshall 大学基金会共同开发的重新设计的人工智能导论课程，标志着 CodePath 的应用 AI 课程首次获得学分认可。

## Broader Commitment
## 更广泛的承诺

This initiative builds on Anthropic's educational work, including partnerships with the American Federation of Teachers, Iceland's Ministry of Education, and Rwanda's government. Anthropic also signed the White House's "Pledge to America's Youth" focused on expanding AI education nationwide.

该倡议建立在 Anthropic 教育工作的基础之上，包括与美国教师联合会、冰岛教育部和卢旺达政府的合作。Anthropic 还签署了白宫"对美国青年的承诺"，致力于在全国范围内扩大 AI 教育。
