# Anthropic Partners with Rwandan Government and ALX to Bring AI Education to Hundreds of Thousands of Learners Across Africa
# Anthropic 与卢旺达政府和 ALX 合作，为非洲数十万学习者带来 AI 教育

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/rwandan-government-partnership-ai-education
> Date: 2025-11-18

---

Anthropic announced a partnership with the Government of Rwanda and African tech training organization ALX to deploy Chidi, an AI learning companion built on Claude, across Africa. The initiative represents "one of the largest AI for education deployments on the continent."

Anthropic 宣布与卢旺达政府和非洲科技培训组织 ALX 合作，在非洲部署基于 Claude 构建的 AI 学习伴侣 Chidi。该倡议代表"非洲大陆上最大规模的 AI 教育部署之一。"

---

## Rwanda's National Education Initiative
## 卢旺达国家教育倡议

The Rwandan government will integrate AI training into its education system, with plans to train up to 2,000 teachers and civil servants. Graduates will receive a year of access to Claude tools, including Claude Pro and Claude Code for developer teams.

卢旺达政府将把 AI 培训整合到其教育系统中，计划培训多达 2,000 名教师和公务员。毕业生将获得一年的 Claude 工具使用权，包括面向开发者团队的 Claude Pro 和 Claude Code。

Paula Ingabire, Rwanda's Minister of ICT & Innovation, emphasized that the collaboration supports the nation's Vision 2050 strategy, enabling the workforce to "engage with emerging technologies responsibly."

卢旺达信息通信技术与创新部长 Paula Ingabire 强调，该合作支持国家的愿景 2050 战略，使劳动力能够"负责任地使用新兴技术。"

---

## Continental Expansion Through ALX
## 通过 ALX 扩展至整个非洲大陆

ALX will bring Chidi to over 200,000 students and young professionals across Africa. The platform functions as a "Socratic mentor," guiding learners through questions rather than providing direct answers, fostering independent problem-solving abilities.

ALX 将把 Chidi 带给非洲各地超过 200,000 名学生和年轻专业人士。该平台作为"苏格拉底式导师"，通过提问而非直接提供答案来引导学习者，培养独立解决问题的能力。

Early results show strong adoption, with learners completing 1,100+ conversations and 4,000 learning sessions since November 4, with 90% positive user feedback.

早期结果显示了强劲的采用率，自 11 月 4 日以来，学习者完成了 1,100 多次对话和 4,000 次学习课程，90% 的用户反馈为正面。

---

## Broader Global Education Strategy
## 更广泛的全球教育战略

This partnership builds on Anthropic's existing education initiatives, including programs in Iceland and partnerships with institutions like the London School of Economics, demonstrating commitment to accessible, responsible AI deployment worldwide.

该合作建立在 Anthropic 现有教育倡议的基础之上，包括在冰岛的项目以及与伦敦政治经济学院等机构的合作，展示了对全球范围内可及、负责任的 AI 部署的承诺。
