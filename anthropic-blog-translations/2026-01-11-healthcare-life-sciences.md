# Advancing Claude in Healthcare and the Life Sciences
# 推进 Claude 在医疗保健和生命科学领域的应用

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/healthcare-life-sciences
> Date: 2026-01-11

---

Anthropic announced expansions to Claude's capabilities for healthcare and life sciences sectors. The company introduced Claude for Healthcare—tools enabling providers, payers, and health tech companies to use Claude through HIPAA-ready products—alongside expanded Claude for Life Sciences features.

Anthropic 宣布扩展 Claude 在医疗保健和生命科学领域的能力。该公司推出了 Claude for Healthcare——使医疗服务提供者、支付方和健康科技公司能够通过符合 HIPAA 要求的产品使用 Claude 的工具——同时扩展了 Claude for Life Sciences 功能。

---

## Model Performance
## 模型表现

Claude Opus 4.5 demonstrates "major forward step" improvements across medical benchmarks, including MedCalc, MedAgentBench, and LatchBio's SpatialBench evaluation.

Claude Opus 4.5 在各项医学基准测试中展现了"重大前进步伐"的改进，包括 MedCalc、MedAgentBench 和 LatchBio 的 SpatialBench 评估。

---

## Healthcare Connectors
## 医疗保健连接器

Claude now integrates with CMS Coverage Database, ICD-10 codes, and the National Provider Identifier Registry, enabling faster prior authorization reviews and claims management.

Claude 现在与 CMS 覆盖数据库、ICD-10 编码和国家提供者标识注册处集成，实现更快的事先授权审查和理赔管理。

---

## Personal Health Integration
## 个人健康集成

Claude Pro and Max subscribers gain secure access to health data via HealthEx, Function, Apple Health, and Android Health Connect—designed to help patients prepare medical conversations.

Claude Pro 和 Max 订阅用户可通过 HealthEx、Function、Apple Health 和 Android Health Connect 安全地访问健康数据——旨在帮助患者准备医疗对话。

---

## Life Sciences Expansion
## 生命科学扩展

New connectors include Medidata (clinical trials), ClinicalTrials.gov, bioRxiv & medRxiv, Open Targets, ChEMBL, and Owkin's Pathology Explorer. Agent Skills support clinical trial protocol drafting and regulatory submissions.

新的连接器包括 Medidata（临床试验）、ClinicalTrials.gov、bioRxiv 和 medRxiv、Open Targets、ChEMBL 以及 Owkin 的 Pathology Explorer。代理技能支持临床试验方案起草和监管提交。

---

## Practical Applications
## 实际应用

Claude assists with: "reviews of prior authorization requests" by cross-referencing coverage requirements, clinical guidelines, and patient records; claims appeals; care coordination; and regulatory document preparation.

Claude 协助完成：通过交叉参考覆盖要求、临床指南和患者记录来"审查事先授权请求"；理赔申诉；护理协调；以及监管文件准备。

## Industry Support
## 行业支持

Major partners including Banner Health, Novo Nordisk, Sanofi, and others emphasized Claude's safety focus and performance capabilities for healthcare workflows.

主要合作伙伴包括 Banner Health、Novo Nordisk、Sanofi 等，都强调了 Claude 在医疗保健工作流程中的安全关注和性能能力。
