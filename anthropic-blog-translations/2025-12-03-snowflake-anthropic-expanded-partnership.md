# Snowflake and Anthropic Announce $200 Million Partnership to Bring Agentic AI to Global Enterprises
# Snowflake 与 Anthropic 宣布 2 亿美元合作，将代理式 AI 带给全球企业

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/snowflake-anthropic-expanded-partnership
> Date: 2025-12-03

---

Anthropic and Snowflake have expanded their strategic partnership with a multi-year, $200 million agreement. This collaboration makes Claude models available to more than 12,600 Snowflake customers across major cloud platforms including Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Azure.

Anthropic 和 Snowflake 通过一项多年、2 亿美元的协议扩大了战略合作伙伴关系。此次合作使 Claude 模型可供超过 12,600 个 Snowflake 客户使用，跨越包括 Amazon Bedrock、Google Cloud Vertex AI 和 Microsoft Azure 在内的主要云平台。

---

## Key Partnership Elements
## 主要合作要素

The agreement establishes a joint go-to-market initiative focused on deploying AI agents across large enterprises. The partnership enables organizations to extract insights from both structured and unstructured data while maintaining security standards.

该协议建立了一项联合市场推广计划，专注于在大型企业中部署 AI 代理。合作使组织能够从结构化和非结构化数据中提取洞察，同时维持安全标准。

Snowflake internally uses Claude extensively. Claude Code enhances developer productivity within Snowflake's engineering teams, while a Claude-powered GTM AI Assistant built on Snowflake Intelligence helps sales teams centralize data and accelerate deal cycles.

Snowflake 内部广泛使用 Claude。Claude Code 提高了 Snowflake 工程团队的开发者生产力，而基于 Snowflake Intelligence 构建的 Claude 驱动的 GTM AI 助手帮助销售团队集中数据并加速交易周期。

---

## Performance Metrics
## 性能指标

Thousands of Snowflake customers already process trillions of Claude tokens monthly through Snowflake Cortex AI. The next phase focuses on deploying AI agents for complex, multi-step analysis. The system achieves "greater than 90% accuracy on complex text-to-SQL tasks based on Snowflake's internal benchmarks."

数千个 Snowflake 客户已通过 Snowflake Cortex AI 每月处理数万亿个 Claude token。下一阶段专注于部署用于复杂多步骤分析的 AI 代理。该系统"基于 Snowflake 内部基准测试，在复杂的文本到 SQL 任务上达到超过 90% 的准确率。"

## Enterprise Applications
## 企业应用

By combining Claude's reasoning capabilities with Snowflake's governed data environment, customers in regulated industries—financial services, healthcare, and life sciences—can transition from pilots to production with confidence.

通过将 Claude 的推理能力与 Snowflake 的受治理数据环境相结合，金融服务、医疗保健和生命科学等受监管行业的客户可以自信地从试点过渡到生产。
