# Mariano-Florentino Cuéllar Appointed to Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust
# Mariano-Florentino Cuéllar 被任命为 Anthropic 长期利益信托基金受托人

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/mariano-florentino-long-term-benefit-trust
> Date: 2026-01-21

---

Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust announced the appointment of Mariano-Florentino (Tino) Cuéllar as a new trustee member. This independent body helps Anthropic fulfill its public benefit mission.

Anthropic 的长期利益信托基金宣布任命 Mariano-Florentino（Tino）Cuéllar 为新的受托人成员。这个独立机构帮助 Anthropic 履行其公共利益使命。

---

## Background on Cuéllar
## Cuéllar 的背景

Cuéllar brings substantial expertise across law, governance, and international relations. His career includes service as a California Supreme Court Justice, leadership at Stanford's Freeman Spogli Institute for International Studies, and his role as Carnegie Endowment for International Peace president. He announced plans to step down from Carnegie in July 2026, returning to Stanford to lead the Center for Advanced Study in the Behavioral Sciences and Knight-Hennessy Scholars Program.

Cuéllar 在法律、治理和国际关系方面拥有丰富的专业知识。他的职业生涯包括担任加利福尼亚最高法院法官、斯坦福大学弗里曼·斯波格利国际研究所所长，以及卡内基国际和平基金会主席。他宣布计划于 2026 年 7 月卸任卡内基的职务，返回斯坦福大学领导行为科学高等研究中心和 Knight-Hennessy 学者项目。

His experience spans three presidential administrations, and he currently chairs the William & Flora Hewlett Foundation board. With roots along the U.S.-Mexico border, his work has consistently examined technology's intersection with public institutions and democratic processes, including co-leading California's AI Frontier Models Working Group with Fei-Fei Li.

他的经历跨越三届总统政府，目前担任 William & Flora Hewlett 基金会董事会主席。他成长于美墨边境，其工作一直关注技术与公共机构和民主进程的交叉，包括与李飞飞共同领导加利福尼亚州 AI 前沿模型工作组。

## Leadership Perspectives
## 领导层观点

Neil Buddy Shah, Trust Chair, emphasized: "As AI becomes a defining factor in geopolitical competition...the Trust needs leaders who understand these dynamics."

信托基金主席 Neil Buddy Shah 强调："随着 AI 成为地缘政治竞争的决定性因素……信托基金需要理解这些动态的领导者。"

Cuéllar stated that "governance structures that marry private sector dynamism with civic responsibility" are increasingly urgent as AI capabilities advance.

Cuéllar 表示，随着 AI 能力的进步，"将私营部门活力与公民责任相结合的治理结构"变得越来越紧迫。

## Departing Trustees
## 离任受托人

Kanika Bahl and Zachary Robinson concluded their trustee terms. Both joined at the Trust's founding in 2023 and significantly contributed to establishing its governance role.

Kanika Bahl 和 Zachary Robinson 结束了他们的受托人任期。两人均在 2023 年信托基金成立时加入，为建立其治理角色做出了重要贡献。
