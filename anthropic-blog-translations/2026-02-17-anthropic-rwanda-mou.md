# Anthropic and the Government of Rwanda Sign MOU for AI in Health and Education
# Anthropic 与卢旺达政府签署人工智能健康与教育谅解备忘录

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-rwanda-mou
> Date: 2026-02-17

---

Anthropic and Rwanda's government formalized a three-year partnership addressing education, healthcare, and public sector modernization. This marks Anthropic's inaugural multi-sector government agreement on the African continent.

Anthropic 与卢旺达政府正式建立了为期三年的合作伙伴关系，涉及教育、医疗保健和公共部门现代化。这标志着 Anthropic 在非洲大陆首次达成多领域政府协议。

---

## Key Partnership Areas
## 主要合作领域

### Health Initiatives
### 健康倡议

The collaboration supports Rwanda's national health objectives, including cervical cancer elimination efforts and reductions in malaria and maternal mortality rates.

该合作支持卢旺达的国家健康目标，包括消除宫颈癌的努力以及降低疟疾和孕产妇死亡率。

---

### Public Sector Development
### 公共部门发展

Government developer teams gain access to Claude and Claude Code, supplemented with training, capacity building, and API credits to advance AI integration across public institutions.

政府开发团队将获得 Claude 和 Claude Code 的使用权限，并辅以培训、能力建设和 API 额度，以推进公共机构的 AI 集成。

---

### Education Expansion
### 教育拓展

The agreement codifies a fall 2025 education partnership providing 2,000 Claude Pro licenses for Rwandan educators, AI literacy training for public servants, and deployment of a Claude-powered learning companion across eight African nations.

该协议将 2025 年秋季的教育合作正式化，为卢旺达教育工作者提供 2,000 个 Claude Pro 许可证、面向公务员的 AI 素养培训，以及在八个非洲国家部署由 Claude 驱动的学习伴侣。

---

## Leadership Perspectives
## 领导层观点

Paula Ingabire, Rwanda's Minister of ICT and Innovation, stated the partnership represents "an important milestone in Rwanda's AI journey," emphasizing designing solutions applicable at national scale.

卢旺达信息通信技术与创新部长 Paula Ingabire 表示，该合作伙伴关系代表了"卢旺达 AI 旅程中的一个重要里程碑"，强调设计适用于国家规模的解决方案。

Elizabeth Kelly, Head of Beneficial Deployments at Anthropic, emphasized that "technology is only as valuable as its reach," highlighting investments in training and capacity building to enable safe, independent AI usage by teachers and health workers.

Anthropic 有益部署负责人 Elizabeth Kelly 强调，"技术的价值取决于其覆盖范围"，并着重指出在培训和能力建设方面的投资，以使教师和卫生工作者能够安全、独立地使用 AI。

---

## Strategic Vision
## 战略愿景

The partnership prioritizes capacity building, responsible deployment, and local autonomy. By investing in skills and institutions, both parties aim to establish foundations for lasting AI value in sectors most impacting people's lives.

该合作伙伴关系优先考虑能力建设、负责任的部署和地方自主权。通过投资技能和机构，双方旨在为对人们生活影响最大的领域建立持久的 AI 价值基础。
