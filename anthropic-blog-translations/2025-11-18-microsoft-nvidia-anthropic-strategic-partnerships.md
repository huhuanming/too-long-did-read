# Microsoft, NVIDIA, and Anthropic Announce Strategic Partnerships
# 微软、NVIDIA 与 Anthropic 宣布战略合作

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/microsoft-nvidia-anthropic-announce-strategic-partnerships
> Date: 2025-11-18

---

Three major technology companies revealed coordinated partnerships to expand Claude's availability and capabilities across cloud infrastructure.

三家主要科技公司宣布了协调的合作伙伴关系，以扩展 Claude 在云基础设施中的可用性和能力。

---

## Azure Scaling
## Azure 扩展

Anthropic committed to purchasing "$30 billion of Azure compute capacity" while contracting additional capacity up to one gigawatt for Claude deployment on Microsoft's platform.

Anthropic 承诺购买"300 亿美元的 Azure 计算容量"，同时签约高达一吉瓦的额外容量用于在微软平台上部署 Claude。

---

## NVIDIA Collaboration
## NVIDIA 合作

The companies established their first deep technology partnership, with Anthropic committing "up to one gigawatt of compute capacity" using NVIDIA Grace Blackwell and Vera Rubin systems for optimized performance.

两家公司建立了首个深度技术合作伙伴关系，Anthropic 承诺使用 NVIDIA Grace Blackwell 和 Vera Rubin 系统提供"高达一吉瓦的计算容量"以实现优化性能。

---

## Investment Commitments
## 投资承诺

"NVIDIA and Microsoft are committing to invest up to $10 billion and up to $5 billion respectively in Anthropic."

"NVIDIA 和微软分别承诺向 Anthropic 投资最高 100 亿美元和最高 50 亿美元。"

---

## Model Access
## 模型访问

Azure customers gain access to frontier Claude models including Sonnet 4.5, Opus 4.1, and Haiku 4.5 through Microsoft Foundry. This arrangement makes Claude "the only frontier model available on all three of the world's most prominent cloud services."

Azure 客户可以通过 Microsoft Foundry 访问包括 Sonnet 4.5、Opus 4.1 和 Haiku 4.5 在内的前沿 Claude 模型。这使 Claude 成为"唯一在全球三大最重要云服务上均可用的前沿模型。"

---

## Microsoft Integration
## 微软集成

Microsoft committed to continuing Claude access across its Copilot ecosystem, including GitHub Copilot, Microsoft 365 Copilot, and Copilot Studio.

微软承诺在其 Copilot 生态系统中继续提供 Claude 访问，包括 GitHub Copilot、Microsoft 365 Copilot 和 Copilot Studio。

---

## Existing Relationships
## 现有关系

The announcement emphasized that "Amazon remains Anthropic's primary cloud provider and training partner."

公告强调，"亚马逊仍然是 Anthropic 的主要云服务提供商和训练合作伙伴。"
