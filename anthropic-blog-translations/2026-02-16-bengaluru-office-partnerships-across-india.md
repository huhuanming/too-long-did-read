# Anthropic Opens Bengaluru Office and Announces New Partnerships Across India
# Anthropic 在班加罗尔开设办公室并宣布印度多项新合作

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/bengaluru-office-partnerships-across-india
> Date: 2026-02-16

---

Anthropic established its second Asian office in Bengaluru, marking a significant expansion into India. The company announced multiple partnerships spanning enterprise, education, and agricultural sectors, led by Managing Director Irina Ghose.

Anthropic 在班加罗尔设立了其在亚洲的第二个办公室，标志着向印度市场的重大扩张。该公司宣布了涵盖企业、教育和农业领域的多项合作伙伴关系，由董事总经理 Irina Ghose 领导。

---

## Market Position
## 市场地位

India represents Anthropic's second-largest Claude.ai market, with usage predominantly focused on computer and mathematical tasks. The company noted that "Nearly half of Claude usage in India comprises computer and mathematical tasks."

印度是 Anthropic Claude.ai 的第二大市场，使用主要集中在计算机和数学任务上。该公司指出，"印度近一半的 Claude 使用涉及计算机和数学任务。"

---

## Language Development
## 语言开发

The organization launched efforts to enhance AI capabilities across ten major Indian languages—Hindi, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu, Tamil, Punjabi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, and Urdu—by curating higher-quality training data.

该组织启动了通过策划更高质量的训练数据来增强十种主要印度语言——印地语、孟加拉语、马拉地语、泰卢固语、泰米尔语、旁遮普语、古吉拉特语、卡纳达语、马拉雅拉姆语和乌尔都语——的 AI 能力的工作。

## Technical Standards
