# Covering Electricity Price Increases from Our Data Centers
# 承担因我们数据中心导致的电价上涨

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/covering-electricity-price-increases
> Date: 2026-02-11

---

Anthropic announced it will "cover electricity price increases that consumers face from our data centers" as the company expands its AI infrastructure investments across America.

Anthropic 宣布将"承担消费者因我们数据中心面临的电价上涨"，同时公司正在扩大其在美国各地的 AI 基础设施投资。

---

## Key Context
## 关键背景

Training advanced AI models requires enormous power consumption. The U.S. AI sector needs "at least 50 gigawatts of capacity over the next several years," making rapid data center development critical for maintaining competitiveness while managing consumer impact.

训练先进的 AI 模型需要巨大的电力消耗。美国 AI 行业"在未来几年至少需要 50 吉瓦的容量"，这使得快速的数据中心建设对于在管理消费者影响的同时保持竞争力至关重要。

---

## Four-Part Commitment
## 四项承诺

### Grid Infrastructure
### 电网基础设施

Anthropic will "pay for 100% of the grid upgrades needed to interconnect" its facilities, covering costs otherwise passed to ratepayers.

Anthropic 将"支付其设施互联所需电网升级的 100% 费用"，承担否则将转嫁给电价支付者的成本。

---

### Power Procurement
### 电力采购

The company pledges to bring new generation capacity online matching its electricity demands and estimate demand-driven price effects where generation lags.

公司承诺将上线与其电力需求匹配的新发电容量，并在发电滞后的情况下估算需求驱动的价格影响。

---

### Grid Optimization
### 电网优化

Investment in curtailment systems reduces peak-demand power usage, while grid optimization tools help lower consumer costs.

对削减系统的投资减少了高峰需求时的电力使用，而电网优化工具有助于降低消费者成本。

---

### Community Investment
### 社区投资

Current projects will create "hundreds of permanent jobs and thousands of construction jobs," with commitments to environmental stewardship and water-efficient cooling technologies.

当前项目将创造"数百个永久性工作岗位和数千个建设工作岗位"，并承诺环境管理和节水冷却技术。

---

## Systemic Solutions
## 系统性解决方案

Anthropic supports federal policies enabling faster energy deployment, acknowledging that "company-level action isn't enough" for keeping electricity affordable long-term.

Anthropic 支持能够加速能源部署的联邦政策，承认"仅靠公司层面的行动不足以"长期保持电力的可负担性。
