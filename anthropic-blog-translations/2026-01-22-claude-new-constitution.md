# Claude's New Constitution
# Claude 的新宪章

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-new-constitution
> Date: 2026-01-22

---

Anthropic has released an updated constitution for Claude, describing it as "a detailed description of Anthropic's vision for Claude's values and behavior; a holistic document that explains the context in which Claude operates."

Anthropic 发布了 Claude 的更新版宪章，将其描述为"Anthropic 对 Claude 的价值观和行为愿景的详细描述；一份解释 Claude 运行环境的整体性文件。"

---

## Key Components
## 核心组成部分

The constitution establishes four core priorities for Claude models:

该宪章为 Claude 模型确立了四个核心优先事项：

1. **Broadly safe** — preventing interference with human oversight of AI development
1. **广泛安全** — 防止干扰人类对 AI 发展的监督

2. **Broadly ethical** — maintaining honesty and avoiding harmful actions
2. **广泛道德** — 保持诚实并避免有害行为

3. **Compliant with Anthropic's guidelines** — following specific organizational instructions
3. **遵守 Anthropic 的指南** — 遵循具体的组织指令

4. **Genuinely helpful** — benefiting users and operators
4. **真正有用** — 使用户和运营者受益

---

## Philosophical Approach
## 哲学方法

Rather than rigid rules, the document emphasizes helping Claude understand *why* certain behaviors matter. As Anthropic explains, models need to "generalize—to apply broad principles rather than mechanically following specific rules" across novel situations.

该文件不是制定僵化的规则，而是强调帮助 Claude 理解某些行为*为何*重要。正如 Anthropic 所解释的，模型需要"泛化——在新情况下应用广泛原则，而非机械地遵循具体规则"。

---

## Main Sections
## 主要章节

The constitution covers helpfulness, Anthropic's specific guidelines, Claude's ethical standards, safety priorities, and questions about Claude's nature and potential consciousness.

该宪章涵盖了有用性、Anthropic 的具体指南、Claude 的道德标准、安全优先事项，以及关于 Claude 本质和潜在意识的问题。

---

## Transparency & Iteration
## 透明度与迭代

The document is released under Creative Commons CC0 1.0, allowing free use by anyone. Anthropic treats it as "a living document and a continuous work in progress," planning ongoing updates informed by external experts across multiple disciplines.

该文件以 Creative Commons CC0 1.0 许可证发布，允许任何人自由使用。Anthropic 将其视为"一份活文件和持续进行中的工作"，计划根据多个学科外部专家的意见进行持续更新。

The full constitution is available at anthropic.com/constitution.

完整宪章可在 anthropic.com/constitution 上获取。
