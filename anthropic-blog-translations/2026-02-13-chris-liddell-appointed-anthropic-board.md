# Chris Liddell Appointed to Anthropic's Board of Directors
# Chris Liddell 被任命为 Anthropic 董事会成员

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/chris-liddell-appointed-anthropic-board
> Date: 2026-02-13

---

Chris Liddell has joined Anthropic's Board of Directors, bringing over three decades of senior leadership experience from major global organizations. His previous roles include Chief Financial Officer at Microsoft, General Motors, and International Paper, as well as Deputy White House Chief of Staff during the Trump administration's first term.

Chris Liddell 加入了 Anthropic 董事会，带来了在全球主要组织超过三十年的高级领导经验。他曾担任微软、通用汽车和国际纸业的首席财务官，以及特朗普政府第一任期的白宫副幕僚长。

---

Daniela Amodei, Anthropic's Co-founder and President, commented: "Chris has spent his career at the intersection of technology, public service, and governance—and has a track record of helping organizations get those things right when the stakes are highest. As AI's impact on society grows, that kind of judgement and experience is exactly what we seek on our board."

Anthropic 联合创始人兼总裁 Daniela Amodei 评论道："Chris 的职业生涯一直处于技术、公共服务和治理的交汇点——他在利害关系最大时帮助组织做出正确决策方面有着良好记录。随着 AI 对社会的影响不断增长，这种判断力和经验正是我们在董事会中所需要的。"

---

Liddell joins existing board members Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, Yasmin Razavi, Jay Kreps, and Reed Hastings. As a Public Benefit Corporation, Anthropic's board is elected by stockholders and the Long-Term Benefit Trust.

Liddell 加入了现有董事会成员 Dario Amodei、Daniela Amodei、Yasmin Razavi、Jay Kreps 和 Reed Hastings 的行列。作为一家公益公司，Anthropic 的董事会由股东和长期利益信托基金选举产生。

---

Liddell stated: "My career has taught me the governance of transformative technologies matters as much as the technologies themselves. Anthropic's approach—building AI that's both capable and responsible—is critical for our future, and it's why I'm proud to be joining the company's Board."

Liddell 表示："我的职业生涯教会我，变革性技术的治理与技术本身同样重要。Anthropic 的方法——构建既有能力又负责任的 AI——对我们的未来至关重要，这也是我为加入公司董事会而自豪的原因。"

---

Currently, Liddell serves as a director at Commonwealth Fusion Systems and the Council on Foreign Relations. He has participated in multiple presidential transition teams and authored a book on the subject. He previously directed the American Technology Council, focusing on government technology modernization.

目前，Liddell 在 Commonwealth Fusion Systems 和外交关系委员会担任董事。他参与了多次总统过渡团队的工作并就此撰写了一本书。他此前曾领导美国技术委员会，专注于政府技术现代化。

Beyond corporate roles, Liddell chairs New Zealand's largest environmental foundation and has served on various nonprofit boards, including the New Zealand Rugby Union. In 2016, he received the Companion of the New Zealand Order of Merit for business and philanthropy contributions.

在企业角色之外，Liddell 担任新西兰最大环境基金会的主席，并在包括新西兰橄榄球联盟在内的多个非营利组织董事会任职。2016 年，他因在商业和慈善方面的贡献获得了新西兰功绩勋章。
