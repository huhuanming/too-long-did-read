# Anthropic and Teach For All Launch Global AI Training Initiative for Educators
# Anthropic 与 Teach For All 启动全球教育者 AI 培训计划

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-teach-for-all
> Date: 2026-01-21

---

Anthropic has partnered with Teach For All to bring AI education to more than 100,000 teachers and alumni across 63 countries. The initiative, called the AI Literacy & Creator Collective (LCC), serves Teach For All's network of educators who work with over 1.5 million students in under-resourced schools.

Anthropic 与 Teach For All 合作，将 AI 教育带给 63 个国家的超过 100,000 名教师和校友。该倡议名为 AI 素养与创作者联盟（LCC），服务于 Teach For All 的教育工作者网络，这些教育工作者在资源不足的学校中服务超过 150 万名学生。

---

## Built on Shared Learning
## 建立在共同学习之上

Rather than positioning teachers as passive tool users, this partnership emphasizes educator agency. Teachers function as co-architects shaping AI development, providing ground-level feedback that informs product evolution.

该合作不是将教师定位为被动的工具使用者，而是强调教育工作者的主体性。教师作为共同设计者参与塑造 AI 的发展，提供来自一线的反馈以推动产品演进。

Wendy Kopp, CEO of Teach For All, stated: "For AI to reach its potential to make education more equitable, teachers need to be the ones shaping how it's used..."

Teach For All 首席执行官 Wendy Kopp 表示："为了让 AI 发挥其使教育更加公平的潜力，教师需要成为塑造其使用方式的人……"

Michael Gilmore, COO of Teach for Australia, noted: "The combination of real-world experience from the Teach For All network and technical insights from Anthropic has provided a fabulous learning opportunity."

Teach for Australia 首席运营官 Michael Gilmore 指出："Teach For All 网络的实际经验与 Anthropic 的技术洞察相结合，提供了一个绝佳的学习机会。"

---

## What Teachers Are Building
## 教师们正在构建的成果

Teachers have created innovative classroom tools using Claude. One educator in Liberia developed an interactive climate education curriculum within weeks of attending training. In Bangladesh, a teacher built a gamified math learning app with boss battles and leaderboards for students struggling with numeracy.

教师们使用 Claude 创建了创新的课堂工具。利比里亚的一位教育工作者在参加培训后几周内就开发了互动式气候教育课程。在孟加拉国，一位教师为数学困难的学生开发了一款游戏化的数学学习应用，包含 Boss 战和排行榜。

Rosina Bastidas, a tech educator in Argentina, stated: "discovering Claude through the community initiative significantly expanded my practice."

阿根廷的技术教育工作者 Rosina Bastidas 表示："通过社区倡议发现 Claude 显著拓展了我的教学实践。"

## Program Structure
## 项目结构

The AI LCC operates through three components:

AI LCC 通过三个组成部分运作：

- **AI Fluency Learning Series:** Six live episodes covering Claude capabilities and classroom applications; over 530 educators attended the inaugural series in November 2025.
- **AI 流利度学习系列：** 六期直播课程，涵盖 Claude 的功能和课堂应用；2025 年 11 月的首期系列有超过 530 名教育工作者参加。

- **Claude Connect:** An ongoing community hub with 1,000+ educators from 60+ countries exchanging resources and discoveries.
- **Claude Connect：** 一个持续运营的社区中心，来自 60 多个国家的 1,000 多名教育工作者在此交流资源和发现。

- **Claude Lab:** An innovation space offering Claude Pro access and direct input into product roadmap development; the program received 200+ applications within four days.
- **Claude Lab：** 一个创新空间，提供 Claude Pro 的使用权限以及对产品路线图开发的直接参与；该项目在四天内收到了 200 多份申请。

## 更广泛的背景

Anthropic's education work extends globally, including AI education pilots in Iceland and partnerships with Rwanda's government and ALX to reach hundreds of thousands of African learners.

Anthropic 的教育工作延伸到全球，包括在冰岛的 AI 教育试点以及与卢旺达政府和 ALX 的合作，以覆盖数十万非洲学习者。
