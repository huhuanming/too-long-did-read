# How Scientists Are Using Claude to Accelerate Research and Discovery
# 科学家如何使用 Claude 加速研究与发现

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/accelerating-scientific-research
> Date: 2026-01-15

---

Anthropic launched Claude for Life Sciences last October, enhancing Claude's capabilities for scientific collaboration. The company has invested in making Claude the most capable model for scientific work, with Opus 4.5 showing improvements in figure interpretation, computational biology, and protein understanding. Through the AI for Science program, Anthropic provides free API credits to leading researchers working on high-impact projects worldwide.

Anthropic 去年十月推出了 Claude for Life Sciences，增强了 Claude 在科学合作方面的能力。该公司一直致力于使 Claude 成为最适合科学工作的模型，Opus 4.5 在图表解读、计算生物学和蛋白质理解方面表现出改进。通过 AI for Science 项目，Anthropic 为全球从事高影响力项目的顶尖研究人员提供免费的 API 额度。

---

## Key Finding
## 关键发现

Scientists have developed custom systems using Claude that extend far beyond literature reviews or coding assistance. Claude now functions as a collaborator across all research stages: determining which experiments to run, compressing multi-month projects into hours, and identifying patterns in massive datasets that humans might miss.

科学家们使用 Claude 开发的定制系统远远超出了文献综述或编程辅助的范畴。Claude 现在作为所有研究阶段的合作伙伴：确定要进行哪些实验，将数月的项目压缩到数小时，并在人类可能遗漏的海量数据集中识别模式。

---

## Case Study: Biomni (Stanford University)
## 案例研究：Biomni（斯坦福大学）

This agentic AI platform consolidates hundreds of tools and databases into one system. In trials, it completed a genome-wide association study in 20 minutes—a task typically requiring months. The system can form hypotheses, design experimental protocols, and work across 25 biological subfields.

这个代理式 AI 平台将数百个工具和数据库整合到一个系统中。在试验中，它在 20 分钟内完成了一项全基因组关联研究——这项任务通常需要数月时间。该系统可以形成假设、设计实验方案，并跨越 25 个生物学子领域工作。

---

## Case Study: Cheeseman Lab (MIT)
## 案例研究：Cheeseman 实验室（MIT）

PhD student Matteo Di Bernardo created MozzareLLM, which automates interpretation of gene knockout experiments. The system identifies biological processes genes share and flags promising research directions. Lab head Iain Cheeseman notes: "Claude consistently catches things I missed" and findings are verifiable discoveries.

博士生 Matteo Di Bernardo 创建了 MozzareLLM，它可以自动解读基因敲除实验。该系统识别基因共享的生物过程并标记有前景的研究方向。实验室负责人 Iain Cheeseman 指出："Claude 持续发现我遗漏的东西"，而且发现是可验证的。

---

## Case Study: Lundberg Lab (Stanford)
## 案例研究：Lundberg 实验室（斯坦福大学）

Rather than relying on intuition for gene selection, this team uses Claude to navigate molecular relationship maps and identify candidate genes based on biological properties, potentially revolutionizing focused perturbation screening approaches.

这个团队不再依赖直觉来选择基因，而是使用 Claude 来导航分子关系图谱，并根据生物学特性识别候选基因，有可能彻底改变定向扰动筛选方法。

## 展望未来

随着 AI 能力的进步，研究人员报告他们的工具变得越来越有用。Claude 每次版本更新的改进展示了其复现科学论文中描述的工作的能力在不断增长。
