# Working with the US Department of Energy to Unlock the Next Era of Scientific Discovery
# 与美国能源部合作开启科学发现的新时代

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/genesis-mission-partnership
> Date: 2025-12-18

---

Anthropic and the US Department of Energy (DOE) announced a multi-year partnership as part of the Genesis Mission—the Department's initiative leveraging AI to strengthen America's scientific leadership. The collaboration targets three key areas: American energy dominance, biological and life sciences, and scientific productivity, with potential impact across all 17 national laboratories.

Anthropic 与美国能源部（DOE）宣布了一项多年合作伙伴关系，作为 Genesis Mission 的一部分——该部门利用 AI 加强美国科学领导力的倡议。合作针对三个关键领域：美国能源主导地位、生物和生命科学以及科学生产力，有可能影响所有 17 个国家实验室。

---

## The Context
## 背景

The Genesis Mission addresses a critical moment where global AI competition intensifies. America must combine its scientific infrastructure—including supercomputers and decades of research data—with frontier AI capabilities to maintain scientific leadership.

Genesis Mission 针对全球 AI 竞争加剧的关键时刻。美国必须将其科学基础设施——包括超级计算机和数十年的研究数据——与前沿 AI 能力相结合，以维持科学领导地位。

---

## Anthropic's Contribution
## Anthropic 的贡献

Anthropic will provide DOE researchers access to Claude and a team of engineers to develop specialized tools:

Anthropic 将为 DOE 研究人员提供 Claude 的使用权限和一个工程团队来开发专用工具：

- AI agents addressing DOE's highest-priority challenges
- 应对 DOE 最高优先级挑战的 AI 代理

- Model Context Protocol servers connecting Claude to scientific instruments
- 将 Claude 连接到科学仪器的模型上下文协议服务器

- Claude Skills for specialized scientific workflows
- 用于专业科学工作流程的 Claude 技能

---

## Application Areas
## 应用领域

**Energy Dominance:** Claude assists with permitting reviews, nuclear technology research, and domestic energy security.

**能源主导地位：** Claude 协助许可审查、核技术研究和国内能源安全。

**Biological Sciences:** The system supports pandemic early-warning systems, biological threat detection, and accelerated drug discovery.

**生物科学：** 该系统支持大流行病早期预警系统、生物威胁检测和加速药物发现。

**Scientific Productivity:** Claude accesses fifty years of DOE research to identify patterns and accelerate research cycles in strategic domains.

**科学生产力：** Claude 访问 DOE 五十年的研究成果，以识别模式并加速战略领域的研究周期。

---

## Future Direction
## 未来方向

Past projects include co-developing a nuclear risk classifier with the National Nuclear Security Administration and expanding Claude deployment at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, establishing a model for human-AI research collaboration.

过去的项目包括与国家核安全管理局共同开发核风险分类器，以及在劳伦斯·利弗莫尔国家实验室扩大 Claude 部署，为人机研究合作建立模式。
