# Anthropic Partners with Allen Institute and Howard Hughes Medical Institute to Accelerate Scientific Discovery
# Anthropic 与艾伦研究所及霍华德·休斯医学研究所合作加速科学发现

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-partners-with-allen-institute-and-howard-hughes-medical-institute
> Date: 2026-02-02

---

Anthropic announced two flagship partnerships designed to enhance scientific research capabilities. "Modern biological research generates data at unprecedented scale" yet faces bottlenecks in converting that data into validated biological insights.

Anthropic 宣布了两项旨在增强科学研究能力的旗舰合作。"现代生物学研究以前所未有的规模产生数据"，但在将这些数据转化为经过验证的生物学见解方面面临瓶颈。

---

## Key Partners
## 主要合作伙伴

The Allen Institute and the Howard Hughes Medical Institute (HHMI) will serve as founding life sciences partners. These collaborations aim to extend Claude's capabilities to frontier scientific research, enabling scientist teams to collaborate more effectively on ambitious challenges.

艾伦研究所和霍华德·休斯医学研究所（HHMI）将担任创始生命科学合作伙伴。这些合作旨在将 Claude 的能力扩展到前沿科学研究，使科学家团队能够更有效地合作应对雄心勃勃的挑战。

---

## HHMI Partnership: Infrastructure for AI-Enabled Discovery
## HHMI 合作：AI 赋能发现的基础设施

HHMI will collaborate with Anthropic through its AI@HHMI initiative, anchored at Janelia Research Campus. The partnership involves developing specialized AI agents for laboratory use that integrate experimental knowledge with cutting-edge instruments and analysis pipelines to accelerate discovery.

HHMI 将通过其 AI@HHMI 计划与 Anthropic 合作，该计划以 Janelia 研究园区为基地。合作涉及开发用于实验室的专用 AI 代理，将实验知识与尖端仪器和分析流程相整合，以加速发现。

---

## Allen Institute Partnership: Multi-Agent Systems
## 艾伦研究所合作：多代理系统

The Allen Institute will develop multi-agent AI systems for multi-modal data analysis. The work explores how specialized AI agents handling "multi-omic data integration, knowledge graph management, temporal dynamics modeling, and experimental design" can coordinate to support scientific investigation.

艾伦研究所将开发用于多模态数据分析的多代理 AI 系统。这项工作探索处理"多组学数据整合、知识图谱管理、时间动态建模和实验设计"的专用 AI 代理如何协调以支持科学调查。

---

## Strategic Approach
## 战略方针

Both partnerships emphasize that AI systems should augment rather than replace human scientific judgment, ensuring insights remain "grounded in evidence and legible to the scientists who use them."

两项合作都强调，AI 系统应当增强而非取代人类的科学判断，确保见解"基于证据且对使用它们的科学家来说是清晰可读的"。
