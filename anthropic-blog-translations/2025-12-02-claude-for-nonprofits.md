# Claude for Nonprofits
# Claude 非营利组织计划

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-nonprofits
> Date: 2025-12-02

---

Anthropic has launched Claude for Nonprofits in partnership with GivingTuesday, aiming to help organizations worldwide amplify their social impact. The initiative addresses a critical challenge: nonprofits often operate with constrained budgets while tackling society's most pressing problems.

Anthropic 与 GivingTuesday 合作推出了 Claude 非营利组织计划，旨在帮助全球组织扩大其社会影响力。该倡议解决了一个关键挑战：非营利组织往往在预算有限的情况下应对社会最紧迫的问题。

---

## Three Core Components
## 三个核心组成部分

The program includes discounted access (up to 75% off), new tool connectors, and educational resources. Nonprofits can access Claude Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.5, and Haiku 4.5 through discounted Team and Enterprise plans tailored for organizational collaboration and security needs.

该计划包括折扣使用权限（最高 75% 折扣）、新的工具连接器和教育资源。非营利组织可以通过为组织协作和安全需求量身定制的团队版和企业版折扣计划使用 Claude Opus 4.6、Sonnet 4.5 和 Haiku 4.5。

---

## New Integrations
## 新集成

Claude now connects to three nonprofit-focused platforms: Benevity (enabling access to 2.4+ million validated organizations), Blackbaud (providing CRM and fundraising capabilities), and Candid (offering nonprofit and funder data for discovery purposes).

Claude 现在连接到三个面向非营利组织的平台：Benevity（可访问超过 240 万个经验证的组织）、Blackbaud（提供 CRM 和筹款功能）和 Candid（提供非营利组织和资助方数据用于发现目的）。

---

## Training and Support
## 培训与支持

A free "AI Fluency for Nonprofits" course helps staff apply AI to grant writing, program evaluation, and donor engagement. Anthropic collaborates with implementation partners including Bridgespan Group, Idealist Consulting, Vera Solutions, and Slalom.

一门免费的"非营利组织 AI 素养"课程帮助工作人员将 AI 应用于拨款申请写作、项目评估和捐赠者参与。Anthropic 与实施合作伙伴合作，包括 Bridgespan Group、Idealist Consulting、Vera Solutions 和 Slalom。

---

## Real-World Applications
## 实际应用

Featured organizations demonstrate measurable results: IDinsight reports 16x faster survey preparation, MyFriendBen achieved 4x faster implementation, and the Epilepsy Foundation deployed a 24/7 AI assistant in five languages serving 3.4 million Americans.

特色组织展示了可衡量的成果：IDinsight 报告调查准备速度提高了 16 倍，MyFriendBen 实现了 4 倍的实施加速，癫痫基金会部署了一个支持五种语言的 24/7 AI 助手，服务 340 万美国人。
