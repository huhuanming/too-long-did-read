# Anthropic Economic Index: New Building Blocks for Understanding AI Use
# Anthropic 经济指数：理解 AI 使用的新构建模块

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/economic-index-primitives
> Date: 2026-01-15

---

Anthropic has released its fourth Economic Index report, introducing "economic primitives"—five foundational measurements to track Claude's economic impact over time. These metrics examine task complexity, skill requirements, use purpose, AI autonomy levels, and task success rates.

Anthropic 发布了其第四份经济指数报告，引入了"经济基元"——五个基础性度量指标，用于追踪 Claude 随时间推移的经济影响。这些指标考察任务复杂性、技能要求、使用目的、AI 自主性水平和任务成功率。

---

## Task Performance
## 任务表现

The research reveals that "more complex tasks were sped up the most by Claude." College-level tasks showed 12x speedup on Claude.ai, compared to 9x for high school-level work. However, success rates decline with complexity—66% for college-level versus 70% for simpler tasks.

研究揭示，"更复杂的任务被 Claude 加速得最多。"大学水平的任务在 Claude.ai 上显示出 12 倍的加速，而高中水平的任务为 9 倍。然而，成功率随复杂性下降——大学水平为 66%，而较简单的任务为 70%。

---

## Time Horizons
## 时间范围

Claude achieves 50% success on 3.5-hour API tasks and 19-hour Claude.ai tasks, substantially longer than benchmark predictions. This difference reflects real-world usage patterns where users break tasks into smaller steps.

Claude 在 3.5 小时的 API 任务和 19 小时的 Claude.ai 任务上达到 50% 的成功率，大大超过基准预测。这一差异反映了现实世界的使用模式，即用户将任务分解为更小的步骤。

---

## Geographic Patterns
## 地理模式

Usage varies dramatically by economic development stage. Wealthier nations use Claude primarily for work and personal tasks, while lower-income countries emphasize educational applications—aligning with an adoption curve pattern.

使用情况因经济发展阶段而异。较富裕的国家主要将 Claude 用于工作和个人任务，而低收入国家则强调教育应用——这与采用曲线模式一致。

---

## Occupational Impact
## 职业影响

While 49% of jobs involve Claude usage, effective coverage weighted by success rates tells a different story. Some roles like data entry and radiology show greater AI impact than task coverage alone suggests. The analysis identifies potential "deskilling" effects, as Claude handles higher-education tasks that currently define many professions.

虽然 49% 的工作涉及 Claude 的使用，但按成功率加权的有效覆盖率讲述了不同的故事。一些角色如数据录入和放射学显示出比单纯任务覆盖所暗示的更大的 AI 影响。分析识别出潜在的"去技能化"效应，因为 Claude 处理的是当前定义许多职业的高等教育任务。

---

## Productivity Estimates
## 生产力估算

Accounting for task reliability reduces earlier productivity projections from 1.8 percentage points annually to 1.0-1.2 percentage points—still significant compared to historical growth rates.

考虑任务可靠性后，早期的生产力预测从每年 1.8 个百分点降至 1.0-1.2 个百分点——与历史增长率相比仍然意义重大。

---

## Conclusion
## 结论

AI adoption remains concentrated geographically and occupationally, with uneven workforce impacts continuing into 2026.

AI 的采用在地理和职业上仍然集中，不均衡的劳动力影响将持续到 2026 年。
