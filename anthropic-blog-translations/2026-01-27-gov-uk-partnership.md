# Anthropic Partners with the UK Government to Bring AI Assistance to GOV.UK Services
# Anthropic 与英国政府合作为 GOV.UK 服务引入 AI 助手

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/gov-UK-partnership
> Date: 2026-01-27

---

Anthropic has been selected by the UK's Department for Science, Innovation and Technology (DSIT) to develop and pilot an AI-powered assistant for GOV.UK. The system will help citizens navigate government services and access tailored guidance.

Anthropic 被英国科学、创新和技术部（DSIT）选中，为 GOV.UK 开发和试点一个 AI 驱动的助手。该系统将帮助公民浏览政府服务并获取定制化的指导。

---

## Initial Focus: Employment Support
## 初始重点：就业支持

The assistant will initially concentrate on helping job seekers by providing personalized career advice, facilitating access to training programs, explaining available supports, and intelligently directing users to relevant services based on individual circumstances.

该助手将最初专注于帮助求职者，提供个性化的职业建议、促进培训项目的获取、解释可用的支持，并根据个人情况智能地将用户引导到相关服务。

---

## Safety-First Approach
## 安全优先的方法

Since signing a memorandum of understanding in February 2025, Anthropic and DSIT have collaborated on safely integrating AI into public services. The GOV.UK assistant represents an agentic system designed to actively guide people through government processes with individualized support rather than simply answering questions.

自 2025 年 2 月签署谅解备忘录以来，Anthropic 和 DSIT 一直在合作将 AI 安全地集成到公共服务中。GOV.UK 助手是一个代理系统，旨在通过个性化支持主动引导人们完成政府流程，而非仅仅回答问题。

---

## Knowledge Transfer
## 知识转移

A key partnership objective involves building AI expertise within the UK government. Anthropic engineers will collaborate with civil servants and Government Digital Service developers throughout the project, ensuring the UK government can eventually maintain the system independently.

合作的一个关键目标是在英国政府内部建立 AI 专业知识。Anthropic 的工程师将在整个项目中与公务员和政府数字服务开发人员合作，确保英国政府最终能够独立维护该系统。

---

## Data Privacy
## 数据隐私

Users retain complete control over their information, including what the system remembers, with the ability to opt out anytime. All personal data is handled in accordance with UK data protection regulations.

用户对其信息保持完全控制，包括系统记住的内容，并可随时选择退出。所有个人数据均按照英国数据保护法规处理。

---

## Phased Implementation
## 分阶段实施

The project follows DSIT's "Scan, Pilot, Scale" framework, allowing for testing, learning, and refinement before broader rollout.

该项目遵循 DSIT 的"扫描、试点、扩展"框架，在更广泛推出之前进行测试、学习和优化。

---

## Broader UK Commitment
## 更广泛的英国承诺

This partnership reflects Anthropic's expanding UK investment and demonstrates commitment to supporting Britain's leadership in AI development.

这一合作反映了 Anthropic 不断扩大的英国投资，并展示了支持英国在 AI 发展中领导地位的承诺。
