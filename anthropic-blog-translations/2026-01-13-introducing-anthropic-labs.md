# Introducing Labs
# 介绍 Labs

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-anthropic-labs
> Date: 2026-01-13

---

Anthropic is expanding its product development approach by establishing Labs, a dedicated team focused on creating experimental products at the frontier of Claude's capabilities.

Anthropic 正在通过成立 Labs 来扩展其产品开发方式，这是一个专注于在 Claude 能力前沿创建实验性产品的专门团队。

The company describes its product strategy as requiring "different motions working in partnership: tinkering and experimenting at the edge of what Claude can do, testing unpolished versions with early users to find what works, and taking what lands and scaling it into products."

该公司将其产品战略描述为需要"不同的行动方式协同合作：在 Claude 能力的边界进行探索和实验，与早期用户测试未完善的版本以找到有效的方案，并将成功的方案规模化为产品。"

---

## Recent Successes
## 近期成功案例

Recent successes from this approach include Claude Code, which achieved billion-dollar status within six months, the Model Context Protocol reaching 100 million monthly downloads as an industry standard, and recent launches of Skills, Claude in Chrome, and Cowork.

这种方法的近期成功案例包括：Claude Code 在六个月内达到了十亿美元的规模，模型上下文协议（MCP）作为行业标准达到每月 1 亿次下载，以及近期推出的 Skills、Claude in Chrome 和 Cowork。

---

## Leadership Changes
## 领导层变动

Leadership changes accompany this expansion. Mike Krieger, Instagram co-founder and former Chief Product Officer at Anthropic, is joining Labs to work alongside Ben Mann. Ami Vora, who joined Anthropic in late 2025, will lead the Product organization while partnering with CTO Rahul Patil.

领导层的变动伴随着此次扩张。Instagram 联合创始人、Anthropic 前首席产品官 Mike Krieger 将加入 Labs，与 Ben Mann 一起工作。2025 年末加入 Anthropic 的 Ami Vora 将领导产品组织，同时与首席技术官 Rahul Patil 合作。

---

## Vision
## 愿景

Daniela Amodei, Anthropic's President, stated: "The speed of advancement in AI demands a different approach to how we build, how we organize, and where we focus. Labs gives us room to break the mold and explore."

Anthropic 总裁 Daniela Amodei 表示："AI 进步的速度要求我们在构建方式、组织方式和关注重点上采取不同的方法。Labs 给了我们打破常规和探索的空间。"

The announcement includes a hiring initiative seeking builders with proven records in creating products people value and thoughtfully shaping emerging technology.

该公告还包括一项招聘计划，寻找在创造人们重视的产品和深思熟虑地塑造新兴技术方面有出色记录的构建者。
