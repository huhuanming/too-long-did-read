# Accenture and Anthropic Launch Multi-Year Partnership to Move Enterprises from AI Pilots to Production
# Accenture 与 Anthropic 启动多年合作，推动企业从 AI 试点走向生产

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-accenture-partnership
> Date: 2025-12-09

---

Anthropic and Accenture announced a major expansion of their partnership aimed at helping enterprises transition from AI pilot programs to full-scale deployment of Claude AI technology.

Anthropic 和 Accenture 宣布大幅扩展合作伙伴关系，旨在帮助企业从 AI 试点项目过渡到 Claude AI 技术的全面部署。

---

## Key Partnership Elements
## 主要合作要素

The companies are establishing the **Accenture Anthropic Business Group**, positioning Anthropic as a select strategic partner with a dedicated practice. Approximately 30,000 Accenture professionals will receive Claude training, creating "one of the largest ecosystems of Claude practitioners in the world."

两家公司正在建立 **Accenture Anthropic 业务集团**，将 Anthropic 定位为拥有专门业务的精选战略合作伙伴。约 30,000 名 Accenture 专业人员将接受 Claude 培训，创建"全球最大的 Claude 从业者生态系统之一。"

Accenture is becoming a premier partner for Claude Code, which holds over half the AI coding market, making the technology available to tens of thousands of developers. The partnership also includes a new joint offering designed to help CIOs measure value and drive AI adoption across engineering organizations.

Accenture 正在成为 Claude Code 的首要合作伙伴，Claude Code 占据了超过一半的 AI 编程市场，使该技术可供数万名开发者使用。合作还包括一项新的联合产品，旨在帮助首席信息官衡量价值并推动工程组织中的 AI 采用。

---

## Industry Solutions
## 行业解决方案

The companies are developing initial solutions for highly regulated sectors including financial services, life sciences, healthcare, and public sector—where strict security and governance requirements are critical.

两家公司正在为金融服务、生命科学、医疗保健和公共部门等高度监管的行业开发初始解决方案——这些行业的严格安全和治理要求至关重要。

## Leadership Quotes
## 领导层引述

CEO Dario Amodei stated that the partnership enables "tens of thousands of Accenture developers will be using Claude Code, making this our largest ever deployment."

首席执行官 Dario Amodei 表示，该合作使"数万名 Accenture 开发者将使用 Claude Code，使其成为我们有史以来最大的部署。"

Accenture's Julie Sweet noted the expansion "will help our clients accelerate the shift from experimenting with AI to using it as a catalyst for reinvention across the enterprise."

Accenture 的 Julie Sweet 指出，这次扩展"将帮助我们的客户加速从 AI 实验转向将其作为企业全面变革的催化剂。"

## Market Position
## 市场地位

Anthropic's enterprise market share has grown from 24% to 40%, according to Menlo Ventures' 2025 State of Generative AI in the Enterprise report.

根据 Menlo Ventures 的 2025 年企业生成式 AI 状况报告，Anthropic 的企业市场份额从 24% 增长到 40%。
