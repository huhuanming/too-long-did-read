# Anthropic Raises $30 Billion in Series G Funding at $380 Billion Post-Money Valuation
# Anthropic 完成 300 亿美元 G 轮融资，投后估值达 3800 亿美元

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-raises-30-billion-series-g-funding-380-billion-post-money-valuation
> Date: 2026-02-12

---

Anthropic secured $30 billion in Series G funding, achieving a $380 billion post-money valuation. The round was led by GIC and Coatue, with co-leadership from D.E. Shaw Ventures, Dragoneer, Founders Fund, ICONIQ, and MGX.

Anthropic 完成了 300 亿美元的 G 轮融资，投后估值达到 3800 亿美元。本轮融资由 GIC 和 Coatue 领投，D.E. Shaw Ventures、Dragoneer、Founders Fund、ICONIQ 和 MGX 联合领投。

---

## Financial Performance
## 财务表现

The company has reached remarkable growth milestones. Less than three years after earning its first revenue dollar, Anthropic now maintains a run-rate revenue of $14 billion, with consistent 10x annual growth over the past three years.

公司已达到了令人瞩目的增长里程碑。在获得第一美元收入后不到三年，Anthropic 目前的年化收入达到 140 亿美元，过去三年保持了持续的 10 倍年度增长。

---

## Customer Expansion
## 客户拓展

Enterprise adoption has accelerated dramatically. The number of customers spending over $100,000 annually on Claude increased sevenfold in the past year. Notably, eight of the Fortune 10 companies now use Claude. Additionally, the count of customers spending over $1 million annually surged from a dozen two years ago to exceeding 500 today.

企业采用急剧加速。过去一年中，每年在 Claude 上花费超过 10 万美元的客户数量增加了七倍。值得注意的是，财富 10 强中有八家公司现在使用 Claude。此外，每年花费超过 100 万美元的客户数量从两年前的十几家激增至如今的 500 多家。

---

## Claude Code Performance
## Claude Code 表现

Claude Code, launched publicly in May 2025, has become a major revenue driver with run-rate revenue exceeding $2.5 billion. Weekly active users have doubled since January 1st. Industry analysis indicates Claude Code authored approximately 4% of GitHub's public commits globally—double the rate from one month prior.

Claude Code 于 2025 年 5 月公开推出，已成为主要收入驱动力，年化收入超过 25 亿美元。自 1 月 1 日以来，周活跃用户翻了一番。行业分析表明，Claude Code 在全球范围内贡献了约 4% 的 GitHub 公共提交——是一个月前的两倍。

## Infrastructure and Expansion
## 基础设施与扩张

The investment supports infrastructure expansion across major cloud platforms: AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure. Anthropic trains Claude on diverse hardware including AWS Trainium chips, Google TPUs, and NVIDIA GPUs, ensuring optimal performance and resilience.

该投资支持在主要云平台——AWS、Google Cloud 和 Microsoft Azure——上的基础设施扩张。Anthropic 在包括 AWS Trainium 芯片、Google TPU 和 NVIDIA GPU 在内的多种硬件上训练 Claude，确保最佳性能和弹性。

---

## Product Innovation
## 产品创新

In January alone, the company launched over thirty products and features, including Cowork, which extends Claude Code's capabilities to broader business applications with eleven open-source plugins.

仅在一月份，公司就推出了超过三十个产品和功能，包括 Cowork，它通过十一个开源插件将 Claude Code 的能力扩展到更广泛的商业应用。
