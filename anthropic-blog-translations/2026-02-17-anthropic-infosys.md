# Anthropic and Infosys Collaborate to Build AI Agents for Telecommunications and Other Regulated Industries
# Anthropic 与 Infosys 合作为电信及其他受监管行业构建 AI 代理

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-infosys
> Date: 2026-02-17

---

Anthropic and Infosys announced a strategic partnership to develop enterprise AI solutions across telecommunications, financial services, manufacturing, and software development. The collaboration integrates Anthropic's Claude models with Infosys Topaz, an AI-first platform leveraging generative and agentic AI technologies.

Anthropic 和 Infosys 宣布建立战略合作伙伴关系，为电信、金融服务、制造和软件开发等领域开发企业 AI 解决方案。该合作将 Anthropic 的 Claude 模型与 Infosys Topaz——一个利用生成式和代理式 AI 技术的 AI 优先平台——进行整合。

---

## Key Partnership Details
## 主要合作细节

India represents Anthropic's second-largest market for Claude.ai, with developers engaged in technically demanding AI work. Nearly half of Claude usage in India involves building applications and shipping production software. Infosys is among the first partners in Anthropic's expanded India presence.

印度是 Anthropic Claude.ai 的第二大市场，开发者们从事着技术要求很高的 AI 工作。印度近一半的 Claude 使用涉及构建应用程序和交付生产软件。Infosys 是 Anthropic 扩大印度业务的首批合作伙伴之一。

---

## Industry Applications
## 行业应用

The collaboration focuses on agentic AI—systems that independently handle multi-step tasks like claims processing, code generation, and compliance reviews. Tailored applications include:

该合作专注于代理式 AI——能够独立处理多步骤任务（如理赔处理、代码生成和合规审查）的系统。定制应用包括：

- **Telecommunications:** Network operations modernization and customer lifecycle management
- **电信：** 网络运营现代化和客户生命周期管理

- **Financial Services:** Risk detection, compliance automation, and personalized customer interactions
- **金融服务：** 风险检测、合规自动化和个性化客户互动

- **Manufacturing & Engineering:** Product design acceleration and R&D timeline reduction
- **制造与工程：** 加速产品设计和缩短研发周期

- **Software Development:** Code writing, testing, and debugging capabilities
- **软件开发：** 代码编写、测试和调试能力

- **Enterprise Operations:** Document summarization and routine workflow automation
- **企业运营：** 文档摘要和日常工作流程自动化

## Executive Perspective
## 高管观点

Dario Amodei, Anthropic's CEO, emphasized: "There's a significant gap between demo-ready models and production systems for regulated industries requiring domain expertise."

Anthropic 首席执行官 Dario Amodei 强调："面向需要领域专业知识的受监管行业，演示就绪的模型与生产系统之间存在巨大差距。"

Infosys CEO Salil Parekh highlighted that the collaboration advances enterprise AI adoption while maintaining intelligence, resilience, and responsibility across modernized financial services and engineering applications.

Infosys 首席执行官 Salil Parekh 强调，该合作推动了企业 AI 的采用，同时在现代化的金融服务和工程应用中保持了智能性、韧性和责任感。
