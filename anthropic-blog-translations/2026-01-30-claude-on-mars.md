# Claude on Mars
# Claude 登陆火星

> Source: https://www.anthropic.com/mars
> Date: 2026-01-30

---

Anthropic's Claude assisted NASA's Perseverance Rover in planning its first AI-directed drive on Mars. On December 8 and 10, 2025, engineers at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) used Claude to chart approximately 400 meters across the Martian surface—a significant milestone in autonomous space exploration.

Anthropic 的 Claude 协助 NASA 的毅力号火星车规划了其首次由 AI 指导的火星驾驶。2025 年 12 月 8 日和 10 日，NASA 喷气推进实验室（JPL）的工程师使用 Claude 在火星表面规划了约 400 米的路线——这是自主太空探索的一个重要里程碑。

---

## The Challenge of Remote Operation
## 远程操作的挑战

Mars communication involves inherent delays. "It takes about twenty minutes for a signal to reach a Mars rover from Earth; by the time a new instruction arrives, the rover will already have acted on the previous one." This limitation necessitates extensive advance planning rather than real-time control.

火星通信存在固有的延迟。"信号从地球到达火星探测器大约需要二十分钟；当新指令到达时，火星车已经在执行上一条指令了。"这一限制要求进行大量的提前规划，而非实时控制。

---

## How Claude Assisted
## Claude 如何协助

JPL engineers provided Claude with years of accumulated rover-driving experience and data. Using Claude Code, the AI analyzed overhead satellite imagery and generated waypoints in Rover Markup Language—the specialized programming system originally developed for earlier Mars missions.

JPL 工程师向 Claude 提供了多年积累的火星车驾驶经验和数据。使用 Claude Code，AI 分析了卫星俯拍图像并以火星车标记语言（Rover Markup Language）生成了航点——这是最初为早期火星任务开发的专用编程系统。

Claude examined images, created ten-meter segments, and iteratively refined its own route proposals. Engineers then ran the plans through simulation software that modeled over 500,000 variables before transmission to Mars.

Claude 检查图像，创建十米路段，并反复优化自己的路线方案。然后工程师们通过模拟软件运行这些计划，在传输到火星之前对超过 500,000 个变量进行建模。

---

## Results and Impact
## 结果与影响

The rover successfully completed the planned route with only minor adjustments needed. Engineers estimate this approach will reduce route-planning time by approximately 50 percent, enabling more frequent drives and expanded scientific data collection.

火星车成功完成了规划的路线，只需要少量调整。工程师估计这种方法将使路线规划时间减少约 50%，从而实现更频繁的行驶和扩展的科学数据收集。

---

## Future Applications
## 未来应用

This demonstration prepares Claude for future missions, including NASA's Artemis program targeting lunar exploration. The autonomous decision-making capabilities displayed could prove invaluable for distant missions with extended communication delays.

这次演示为 Claude 参与未来任务做了准备，包括 NASA 针对月球探索的阿尔忒弥斯计划。所展示的自主决策能力对于通信延迟较长的远距离任务可能具有极其重要的价值。
