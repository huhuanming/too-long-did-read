# 介绍 Claude Haiku 4.5
# Introducing Claude Haiku 4.5

> 原文链接: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-haiku-4-5
> 日期: 2025-10-15

---

## English

Anthropic has released Claude Haiku 4.5, a compact model delivering remarkable capabilities. The company describes it as matching state-of-the-art performance from just five months ago while operating at significantly reduced costs and faster speeds.

### Key Performance Metrics

The new model achieves approximately one-third the pricing of its predecessor Claude Sonnet 4 while executing tasks more than twice as quickly. On SWE-bench Verified—a benchmark measuring real-world coding performance—Haiku 4.5 reached 73.3% accuracy. The model even surpasses Sonnet 4 at specific tasks like computer interaction.

### Pricing and Availability

Developers can access Claude Haiku 4.5 through the Claude API using the identifier `claude-haiku-4-5`. Pricing stands at $1/$5 per million input and output tokens. The model is now available on Claude Code, Amazon Bedrock, and Google Cloud's Vertex AI.

### Safety Assessment

Testing demonstrated low rates of concerning behaviors and substantially improved alignment compared to Claude Haiku 3.5. The model received classification under AI Safety Level 2 (ASL-2)—less restrictive than the ASL-3 standard applied to Sonnet 4.5 and Opus 4.1—due to limited risks regarding weapons production.

### Use Cases

The offering targets applications requiring responsiveness: chat assistants, customer service systems, pair programming, and multi-agent orchestration projects benefit from the speed-to-intelligence balance.

## 中文翻译

Anthropic 发布了 Claude Haiku 4.5，一个提供卓越能力的紧凑型模型。公司将其描述为匹配仅五个月前的最先进性能，同时以显著降低的成本和更快的速度运行。

### 关键性能指标

新模型的定价约为其前代 Claude Sonnet 4 的三分之一，同时执行任务的速度提高了两倍以上。在 SWE-bench Verified——一个衡量真实世界编程性能的基准测试中——Haiku 4.5 达到了 73.3% 的准确率。该模型甚至在计算机交互等特定任务上超越了 Sonnet 4。

### 定价和可用性

开发者可以通过 Claude API 使用标识符 `claude-haiku-4-5` 访问 Claude Haiku 4.5。定价为每百万输入和输出 token 1 美元/5 美元。该模型现已在 Claude Code、Amazon Bedrock 和 Google Cloud 的 Vertex AI 上提供。

### 安全评估

测试表明令人担忧的行为发生率较低，与 Claude Haiku 3.5 相比对齐性有了显著改善。由于在武器生产方面的风险有限，该模型被归类为 AI 安全级别 2 (ASL-2)——比适用于 Sonnet 4.5 和 Opus 4.1 的 ASL-3 标准限制更少。

### 使用场景

该产品面向需要响应速度的应用：聊天助手、客户服务系统、配对编程和多代理编排项目都受益于其速度与智能的平衡。
